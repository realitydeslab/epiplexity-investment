# ARM Holdings (ARM) — Leader Profile 👤

**Date:** March 6, 2026

---

## CEO: Rene Haas

- **Background:** Former NVIDIA executive (SVP, Computing Products). Joined Arm in 2013, became CEO in February 2022.
- **Track record:** Successfully pivoted Arm from pure mobile licensing to AI/data center/automotive diversification. Navigated post-NVIDIA-merger-collapse IPO in Sept 2023.
- **Strategic vision:** "Up the stack" — moving from basic architecture licensing to Compute Subsystems (CSS), commanding 2-3x royalty rates. Vertical focus: Cloud, Automotive, AI.
- **Execution grade:** A-. Revenue growth accelerated under his leadership (26% YoY in Q3 FY2026). Successfully repositioned the company narrative.

## CFO: Jason Child

- **Background:** Former Opendoor and Splunk CFO. Joined Arm 2023.
- **Notable:** Quantified worst-case smartphone headwind as only 2% royalty impact — effectively de-risked the mobile bear case.
