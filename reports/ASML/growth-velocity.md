# ASML — Growth Velocity 📈

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Revenue Trajectory

| Period | Revenue | YoY Growth | Notes |
|--------|---------|------------|-------|
| FY 2023 | €27.6B | +30% | Strong EUV cycle |
| FY 2024 | €28.3B | +3% | Mid-cycle pause, China restrictions |
| FY 2025 | €32.7B | +16% | Record year, AI capex boom |
| FY 2026E | €34-39B | +4% to +19% | Guided range, High-NA ramp |

## Quarterly Trend (2025)

| Quarter | Revenue | Bookings | Gross Margin |
|---------|---------|----------|--------------|
| Q1 2025 | ~€7.5B | ~€3.6B | ~51% |
| Q2 2025 | ~€7.5B | ~€5.6B | ~51% |
| Q3 2025 | ~€7.5B | ~€2.6B | ~50% |
| Q4 2025 | ~€10.2B | €13.2B | ~52% |

**Key takeaway:** Q4 2025 was a breakout. €13.2B in bookings is the strongest quarter EVER. Revenue acceleration is real — Q4 was 36% above the Q1-Q3 average.

## Growth Acceleration Analysis

- **Revenue:** ACCELERATING. After a flat 2024, 2025 showed +16% with a hockey-stick Q4. 2026 guidance midpoint (~€36.5B) implies +12% growth.
- **Bookings:** STRONGLY ACCELERATING. Q4 2025 bookings (€13.2B) = more than Q1-Q3 combined. 56% were EUV orders.
- **ASP trend:** ACCELERATING. High-NA machines at $350M vs $200M for standard EUV = massive ASP uplift per system.
- **Installed base services:** Growing steadily at ~€6B/year — recurring revenue base.

## Guidance vs. Actual History

ASML has a pattern of **guiding conservatively and beating.** The €34-39B range for 2026 is wide, which suggests upside potential. In Jan 2025, they guided €30-35B for 2025 and delivered €32.7B (above midpoint).

## Path to Profitability

Already highly profitable:
- **Gross margin target:** 51-53% for 2026
- **Net income 2025:** ~€8.5B
- **FCF generation:** Strong, funding €6B+ buyback from operating cash flow

## Unit Economics

- Monopoly pricing power on EUV/High-NA — no competitor within a decade
- Installed base services growing as fleet expands (razor/blade model)
- R&D spend ~€4B/year but customer co-funding offsets portion

## Verdict

**Growth is accelerating on all metrics that matter.** The combination of High-NA volume shipments ($350M/unit), record bookings, and AI-driven capex creates a multi-year revenue acceleration cycle. The only headwind is the ~€3B China DUV decline, which is temporary and well-guided.
