# ASML — Catalyst Map 🎯

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## 1. Q1 2026 Earnings (April 2026)

- **What:** First quarter under new €34-39B revenue guidance. Market watching for High-NA revenue ramp and China DUV headwind impact.
- **When:** Late April 2026
- **Impact:** ±10-15% move if beat/miss. Bookings trajectory is the key number — after €13.2B record Q4.
- **Probability of beat:** High — backlog is massive, orders already secured via prepayments.
- **Market expectation:** Slight sequential decline from blockbuster Q4 2025. Consensus ~€8-9B revenue.
- **Our edge:** High-NA shipments at $350M/unit create lumpy but powerful revenue. Even 1-2 extra units meaningfully beats.

## 2. High-NA EUV Volume Ramp (Throughout 2026)

- **What:** ASML began high-volume shipments of Twinscan EXE:5200 High-NA systems ($350M each) to Intel and Samsung in Feb 2026. TSMC expected to follow. This is the "Angstrom Era" catalyst.
- **When:** Ongoing — each shipment is a revenue event
- **Impact:** Each machine = €320M+ revenue. 8-12 machines in 2026 = €2.5-4B incremental revenue.
- **Probability:** High — machines already shipping, fabs being built
- **Market expectation:** Priced in at guidance level, but upside if adoption accelerates
- **Our edge:** High-NA enables single-exposure patterning at sub-2nm. This isn't optional for leading-edge chipmakers — it's mandatory. Demand pull is structural, not cyclical.

## 3. TSMC High-NA Orders (Q2-Q3 2026)

- **What:** TSMC, the largest ASML customer, placing formal High-NA orders for N2P/A16 process nodes.
- **When:** Expected formal announcement Q2-Q3 2026
- **Impact:** +5-8% on confirmation. Validates High-NA total addressable market.
- **Probability:** High — TSMC roadmap requires it
- **Market expectation:** Largely expected but not fully priced

## 4. China Revenue Headwind — Better Than Feared? (H1 2026)

- **What:** ASML guided ~€3B less revenue from China in 2026 due to DUV pull-forward buying in 2025.
- **When:** Throughout H1 2026
- **Impact:** If China decline is less severe, stock re-rates +5-10%
- **Probability of better-than-feared:** Medium
- **Our edge:** Consensus too bearish on China — domestic fabs still need maintenance/upgrades

## 5. Share Buyback Program (2026)

- **What:** Active buyback announced Jan 28, 2026. ~€25M/day (~€6B+ annualized).
- **When:** Ongoing
- **Impact:** 1-2% EPS accretion + price floor
- **Probability:** High — already executing

## 6. US/EU Fab Subsidies & Domestic Demand

- **What:** CHIPS Act + EU Chips Act driving new fab construction requiring ASML tools.
- **When:** Orders placed throughout 2026 for 2027-2028 delivery
- **Impact:** Extends backlog visibility

## 7. Export Restriction Escalation Risk

- **What:** Further US/Dutch restrictions on DUV sales to China
- **When:** Unpredictable
- **Impact:** -5-10% on new restrictions
- **Probability:** Medium
