# ASML — Investment Thesis

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

---

## The Catalyst

**High-NA EUV volume shipments have begun.** ASML started delivering $350M Twinscan EXE:5200 machines to Intel and Samsung in February 2026, with TSMC orders expected Q2-Q3. This is a generational transition — like DUV→EUV in 2018, but with 75% higher ASPs. Record Q4 2025 bookings of €13.2B (strongest quarter ever) confirm demand is real and accelerating.

## The Trade

- **Entry:** $1,350-1,480 (current ~$1,480, better on pullback)
- **12-month target:** $1,650 (base), $1,900 (bull)
- **Stop-loss:** $1,240 (below active buyback zone)
- **Position size:** 5-8% of portfolio

## The Risk

1. **China revenue headwind** (~€3B less in 2026 — already guided, likely priced in)
2. **Export restriction escalation** (new US/Dutch rules could further cut China sales)
3. **Semiconductor capex cycle peak** (if AI spending slows, ASML orders get deferred)
4. **High-NA yield issues** (if customer adoption is slower than expected)

## The Verdict

**BUY.** ASML is a monopoly compounder entering a multi-year upgrade cycle. At ~24x forward earnings, you're paying a reasonable premium for:
- 100% monopoly in EUV lithography (and 100% in High-NA)
- Revenue growth accelerating from +3% (2024) → +16% (2025) → +12-19% (2026E)
- Record bookings providing 12-18 month revenue visibility
- Active €6B+ buyback demonstrating management confidence
- Structural demand from AI capex that is INCREASING, not plateauing

The moat is the strongest in tech. The growth is accelerating. The valuation is reasonable. Buy on pullbacks, hold for the cycle.
