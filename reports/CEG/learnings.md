# CEG Structured Intelligence — Constellation Energy

## Sector/Market Intelligence
- **Nuclear renaissance is real and accelerating.** Government policy (both parties), hyperscaler demand, and energy security concerns all converge on nuclear. CEG sits at the center.
- **AI data center power demand is the defining energy theme of 2025-2030.** Every major tech company needs gigawatts of reliable, carbon-free power. Nuclear is the only proven technology that delivers this.
- **The Calpine deal signals industry consolidation.** Expect more M&A in power generation as companies scale to meet AI demand.

## Contrarian Angle
- **What the market is missing:** The Calpine deal is being treated as a traditional utility acquisition. It's actually a strategic transformation — CEG becomes the only company offering nuclear + gas + geothermal at scale. This combination is worth more than the sum of parts.
- **Underappreciated risk:** CEG's FY2025 EPS decline (-38%) has spooked some investors. This is noise, not signal — one-time costs and mark-to-market effects. The underlying business is stronger than ever.
- **The "boring utility" discount:** CEG trades like a growth stock to some, but many utility-focused investors won't pay 28x forward PE. This creates a valuation gap that could close as the investor base shifts.
