# CEG Catalyst Map — Constellation Energy

## 1. Calpine Acquisition Closure (~H2 2026)
- **What:** $16.4B acquisition of Calpine (natural gas + geothermal), creating America's largest clean energy company. $26.6B including debt.
- **When:** Expected H2 2026 (regulatory approvals from FERC, state PUCs pending)
- **Impact:** +15-25% upside. Adds ~26 GW of nat gas/geothermal capacity to CEG's ~32 GW nuclear fleet. Diversifies revenue, adds gas peaking assets critical for grid reliability. Accretive to earnings immediately.
- **Probability:** High (70-80%). No major antitrust concerns — complementary portfolios (nuclear vs. gas).
- **Market expectation:** Priced in at ~60%. Market skeptical on regulatory timeline.
- **Our edge:** Deal creates a unique clean energy + reliability portfolio that no competitor can replicate. Regulatory risk is overstated — bipartisan support for energy security.

## 2. Three Mile Island Unit 1 Restart (2028 target, but 2026 milestones)
- **What:** Microsoft 20-year PPA to restart TMI Unit 1 (837 MW). $1B federal loan secured Nov 2025. NRC relicensing process underway.
- **When:** NRC license renewal application likely in 2026; full restart ~2028. Key 2026 milestones: engineering assessments, equipment orders, NRC engagement.
- **Impact:** +5-10% on positive NRC milestones. Long-term: ~$1B+ annual revenue from Microsoft PPA at premium prices.
- **Probability:** Medium-High (65%). Technical and regulatory risk, but strong government support.
- **Market expectation:** Market treats this as 2028+ optionality, underweighting near-term progress.
- **Our edge:** Each milestone de-risks the project. NRC has strong incentive to approve given energy security narrative.

## 3. Nuclear Uprate Programs (Ongoing through 2026)
- **What:** Upgrading existing nuclear plants to produce ~1.5 GW more power without building new reactors.
- **When:** Rolling through 2026-2027.
- **Impact:** +3-5%. Each 100 MW uprate = ~$50-80M annual revenue at current power prices.
- **Probability:** High (85%). NRC-approved process, low technical risk.

## 4. AI Data Center Power Contracts (2026 ongoing)
- **What:** Beyond Microsoft, additional hyperscaler PPAs for nuclear power. Meta mentioned by CEO Dominguez in Jan 2026 Barron's interview.
- **When:** Announcements possible any quarter in 2026.

- **Probability:** High (75%). CEG owns the largest nuclear fleet in the US — hyperscalers need 24/7 carbon-free power.
- **Our edge:** CEG is the ONLY company that can offer gigawatt-scale 24/7 carbon-free power.
