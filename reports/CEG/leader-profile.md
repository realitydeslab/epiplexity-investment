# CEG Leader Profile — Constellation Energy

## CEO: Joseph Dominguez
- **Role:** President & CEO since 2022 spin-off from Exelon
- **Background:** Exelon veteran, deep utility/nuclear expertise, led Constellation through the Exelon separation
- **Execution track record:** Strong. Navigated spin-off successfully, secured Microsoft TMI deal (Sep 2024), orchestrated $16.4B Calpine acquisition (Jan 2025), obtained $1B federal loan (Nov 2025)
- **Strategic vision:** Positioned CEG as THE nuclear-powered AI energy company. Pivoted from pure utility to AI infrastructure play.
- **Public statements:** Jan 2026 Barron's interview — discussed Calpine deal rationale, Meta partnership work, bullish on EV charging demand. Confident and articulate.
- **Grade:** A-. Right leader for this moment — understands both nuclear operations and the AI energy opportunity.

Dominguez has executed on every major promise since the 2022 spin-off. The Microsoft TMI deal was a stroke of genius — it created the "nuclear for AI" narrative that has driven the stock from ~$25 (2022) to $319. The Calpine acquisition shows he's thinking about scale and diversification, not just riding one narrative. This is a CEO who knows how to create and capture value.
