# Nebius Group (NBIS) — Competitive Map

## Direct Competitors (Independent GPU Cloud)

### CoreWeave (CRWV)
- **Status:** Largest direct competitor. IPO'd recently. Microsoft's biggest GPU cloud customer.
- **Strength:** Scale, hyperscaler relationships, first-mover in GPU-as-a-service
- **Weakness:** Reports of Microsoft contract renegotiation rumors (denied). Higher pricing than Nebius.
- **If NBIS wins:** CoreWeave could lose share of incremental hyperscaler contracts
- **Pair trade:** Long NBIS / Short CRWV if you believe Nebius executes better at lower cost

### Lambda
- **Status:** Developer/research-focused GPU cloud. Smaller scale than Nebius.
- **Strength:** Strong brand with AI researchers, free egress, per-minute billing
- **Weakness:** Availability issues, smaller infrastructure footprint
- **Differentiation from NBIS:** Lambda serves smaller customers; Nebius targets hyperscaler-scale contracts

### RunPod
- **Status:** Per-second billing GPU cloud for inference/burst workloads
- **Strength:** Cheapest H100 pricing ($1.99/hr PCIe), flexible billing
- **Weakness:** Not suited for enterprise/hyperscaler contracts
- **Impact:** Competes at the long tail, not for Nebius's core Microsoft/Meta-scale deals

## Hyperscaler Competitors

### AWS, Azure, GCP
- **Key dynamic:** These are BOTH customers AND competitors of Nebius
- Microsoft is Nebius's largest customer ($17.4B deal) but also competes via Azure
- This is the same dynamic as CoreWeave — hyperscalers outsource overflow capacity to GPU cloud providers
- **Risk:** If hyperscalers build enough internal capacity, they stop outsourcing
- **Counter:** AI demand growth is outpacing internal buildout — independent providers fill the gap

## Who Benefits from the SAME Catalysts?
- **NVIDIA (NVDA):** Every dollar Nebius spends on capex flows to NVIDIA GPUs. NBIS's $16–20B 2026 capex is directly bullish for NVDA.
- **Power/Energy (VST, CEG):** Nebius's 2GW+ contracted power demand is bullish for nuclear/energy plays
- **Data center construction (FLR, CLS):** Nebius building multiple mega-campuses
- **Networking (ANET):** AI cluster networking demand rises with Nebius expansion
- **Corning (GLW):** Fiber optic connectivity for data centers

## Who Loses if NBIS Wins?
- **CoreWeave (CRWV):** Direct share loss in hyperscaler overflow market
- **Traditional cloud resellers:** Companies marking up AWS/Azure GPU instances
- **Smaller GPU clouds (Vast.ai, etc.):** Can't compete on scale or contract size

## Pair Trade Opportunities
1. **Long NBIS / Short CRWV** — Bet on Nebius's cost advantage and Microsoft relationship
2. **Long NBIS + NVDA** — Double exposure: picks + shovels + the mine itself
3. **Long NBIS + VST/CEG** — AI infrastructure + the power that feeds it
