# TSLA Catalyst Map — 12-Month Horizon (March 2026 → March 2027)

## 1. Robotaxi Expansion to 7+ New Markets (H1 2026)
- **What:** Tesla expanding unsupervised robotaxi from Austin to Dallas, Houston, Phoenix, Miami, Orlando, Tampa, Las Vegas
- **When:** H1 2026 (rolling, already in progress)
- **Impact:** +15-25% on successful multi-city launch — proves scalability beyond single geofence
- **Probability:** HIGH — Austin already live since Jan 2026, BofA confirms 7 new cities H1 2026
- **Market expectation:** Priced in partially — consensus expects 3-4 cities, not all 7
- **Our edge:** Tesla's camera-only approach scales cheaper than Waymo's LiDAR. Each new city is incremental proof

## 2. CyberCab Volume Production (April 2026)
- **What:** Dedicated robotaxi vehicle (no steering wheel/pedals) begins production at Giga Texas
- **When:** April 2026 production start, consumer sales at $30K announced for 2026
- **Impact:** +10-20% — transforms narrative from "cars with FSD" to "purpose-built autonomous fleet"
- **Probability:** HIGH — first unit already off the line (Feb 2026), Musk confirmed consumer sales this year
- **Market expectation:** Street skeptical on timeline
- **Our edge:** Production has already started. This isn't a promise anymore

## 3. NHTSA FSD Data Deadline (March 9, 2026)
- **What:** NHTSA required Tesla to submit comprehensive FSD safety data
- **When:** March 9, 2026 (2 days away)
- **Impact:** -10-15% if negative regulatory action; +5-10% if data validates safety
- **Probability:** MEDIUM — binary event
- **Market expectation:** Nervousness priced in — stock pulled back from $498 highs
- **Our edge:** Tesla has >1M FSD subscribers generating massive safety data. Data likely favorable

## 4. Fremont → Optimus Robot Factory (Q2 2026)
- **What:** Model S/X production ending; Fremont converting to 1M-unit-capacity Optimus humanoid robot factory
- **When:** Q2 2026 (confirmed Q4 2025 earnings call)
- **Impact:** +10-30% if credible ramp — opens $10T+ TAM narrative
- **Probability:** MEDIUM — conversion happening, 1M units is aspirational
- **Market expectation:** Most analysts value Optimus at $30B+ but treat as 2028+ story
- **Our edge:** Market underestimates Fremont conversion speed once S/X dies

## 5. FSD Subscription Revenue Inflection
- **What:** 1.1M paid FSD customers, full subscription transition; adoption accelerating
- **When:** Ongoing, inflection Q2-Q3 2026
- **Impact:** +5-10% per quarter of acceleration — high-margin recurring revenue
- **Probability:** HIGH — already at 1.1M subscribers
- **Market expectation:** Subscription headwinds known; upside from normalization not priced
- **Our edge:** Once subscription normalizes → software-margin business

## 6. Energy Business Breakout
- **What:** Energy hit $12.8B revenue FY2025 (+26.6% YoY), record MegaPack/Powerwall deployments
- **When:** Each quarterly earnings
- **Impact:** +3-5% per strong quarter — re-rates Tesla as energy company
- **Probability:** HIGH — massive backlog, proven execution
- **Market expectation:** BofA values at $90B. Increasingly recognized

## 7. Q1 2026 Earnings (Late April)
- **What:** First quarter with robotaxi revenue data, CyberCab production updates
- **When:** Late April 2026
- **Impact:** +/-10-15%
- **Probability:** HIGH (event certain, direction unclear)
- **Our edge:** Robotaxi monetization data disclosed first time — potential re-rating

## 8. Macro: Rate Cuts & Political Risk
- **What:** Fed cuts help EV financing; Musk/DOGE political backlash creating brand headwinds
- **When:** Throughout 2026
- **Impact:** +5-10% rate tailwind; -5-10% brand damage risk
