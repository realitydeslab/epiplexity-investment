# TSLA Structured Intelligence & Learnings

## What This Tells Us About the Sector
1. **The "Auto 2.0" transition is real:** Tesla's pivot from car sales to autonomous mobility validates that the $10T+ mobility-as-a-service TAM is being contested NOW, not in 2030
2. **Energy storage is the quiet revolution:** Tesla Energy at $12.8B is bigger than most standalone energy companies. The AI data center power boom is a massive tailwind
3. **Camera-only vs. LiDAR is becoming THE debate:** If Tesla proves camera-only works at scale, the entire autonomous industry restructures around cheaper perception stacks
4. **The EV price war has a floor:** Tesla's margin recovery Q3→Q4 2025 (15.4% → 17.9%) suggests the worst of the BYD-driven price war may be over

## Adjacent Plays
- **NVDA** — supplies AI training compute for FSD and Optimus neural nets
- **CEG/VST** — power for both AI data centers AND Tesla Supercharger/Megapack ecosystems
- **UBER** (as a short) — most exposed to robotaxi disruption
- **Figure AI** (private) — if Optimus validates humanoid TAM, Figure benefits from sector re-rating
- **Mobileye (MBLY)** — alternative autonomous play, supplier model, lower risk/lower reward

## Contrarian Angle — What's the Market Missing?
1. **Brand damage is temporary, autonomy is permanent:** The Musk/DOGE backlash is dominating sentiment, but if robotaxi works, nobody cares about the CEO's politics when the ride is $0.25/mile
2. **Energy is undervalued at $90B:** If energy maintains 25%+ growth, it alone could be worth $150B+ in 2-3 years
3. **FSD subscriptions are a SaaS business hidden inside a car company:** 1.1M subscribers × $99-199/month = massive recurring revenue at software margins
4. **CyberCab at $30K changes the ownership model:** Not just a robotaxi — it's the cheapest Tesla ever, and it drives itself. Consumer demand could surprise

## What Could Go Wrong (Bear Risks)
1. **Regulatory crackdown** — NHTSA, state-level bans, Europe refusing FSD approval
2. **Fatal accidents** — one high-profile unsupervised robotaxi fatality could pause entire program
3. **Musk distraction deepens** — if DOGE role expands or new controversies emerge
4. **China competition** — BYD + Baidu autonomous combo could lock Tesla out of world's largest market
5. **Valuation compression** — at 196x forward P/E, any macro shock or rate hike sends this down 30%+
