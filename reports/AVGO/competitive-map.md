# AVGO Competitive Map — Broadcom

## Direct Competitors

### Marvell Technology (MRVL)
- Custom AI ASICs for Amazon (Trainium), Microsoft
- Threat: Medium — smaller scale, fewer design wins

### NVIDIA (NVDA)
- Cooperative AND competitive — GPUs vs. custom ASICs
- NVIDIA could enter custom ASIC market directly (rumored)
- Broadcom wins on cost efficiency for inference at scale

### In-House Chip Efforts
- Google, Meta could internalize more design — but 3-5 year switching cycles protect Broadcom

## Same-Catalyst Beneficiaries
- MRVL — custom ASIC demand rising for all
- ANET — AI networking parallels Broadcom's
- TSM — manufactures Broadcom's chips
- ASML — EUV tools needed for production

## Losers if Broadcom Wins
- NVDA (partially) — custom ASICs take inference share
- INTC — loses networking and enterprise share

## Pair Trades
1. Long AVGO / Short NVDA — custom ASIC vs. GPU bet
2. Long AVGO + MRVL basket — custom silicon sector
3. Long AVGO / Short INTC — new vs. legacy

## Moat: Deep and Widening
- Multi-year co-engineering (3-5 year lock-in)
- Dominant Ethernet networking for AI
- VMware enterprise software lock-in
- $8B/quarter FCF enables R&D dominance
