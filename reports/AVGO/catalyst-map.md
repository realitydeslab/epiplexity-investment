# AVGO Catalyst Map — Broadcom (March 2026)

## 1. Q2 FY2026 Earnings (June 2026)
- **What:** Q2 results expected to show ~$22B revenue (guided above $20.5B consensus)
- **When:** Early June 2026
- **Impact:** +10-15% if AI revenue acceleration continues; guided 68% adj. EBITDA margin (above 66% consensus)
- **Probability:** High — management has consistently beaten guidance
- **Market expectation:** $20.5B revenue was old consensus; now recalibrated to ~$22B after Q1 guide
- **Our edge:** Market may still underestimate AI revenue ramp — Q1 AI revenue was $8.4B (+106% YoY), "above forecast" per CEO. If Q2 AI hits $10B+, stock reprices higher

## 2. Custom ASIC Expansion — 4th and 5th Hyperscaler Customers
- **What:** Broadcom confirmed ramp of new custom AI chip customers beyond Google, Meta, and likely ByteDance. Apple and Anthropic are speculated
- **When:** Announcements expected throughout 2026
- **Impact:** +15-20% — each new hyperscaler customer adds $5-10B in long-term TAM
- **Probability:** High — CEO Tan confirmed "secured supply chain" for $100B+ AI revenue by 2027
- **Market expectation:** 3 confirmed customers priced in; 4th/5th would be upside surprise
- **Our edge:** Gemini 3 on Ironwood TPUs (Broadcom-designed) caused "Code Red" at OpenAI — validates custom ASIC thesis

## 3. $100B+ AI Chip Revenue Target for 2027
- **What:** CEO Hock Tan projected "significantly in excess of $100 billion" in AI chip sales by 2027
- **When:** Revenue trajectory visible through FY2026 quarterly results
- **Impact:** +20-30% if credibility builds each quarter — implies ~2.5x from current ~$35B annual AI run-rate
- **Probability:** Medium-High — supply chain secured per CEO, but execution risk remains
- **Market expectation:** Some skepticism at current multiples; consensus still below $100B
- **Our edge:** Broadcom has a history of under-promising and over-delivering under Tan

## 4. $10 Billion Share Buyback Program
- **What:** New buyback authorization announced March 4, 2026
- **When:** Through 2027, likely front-loaded
- **Impact:** +3-5% EPS accretion; floor under stock price
- **Probability:** Very High — Broadcom is a disciplined capital allocator
- **Market expectation:** Priced in but provides downside protection
- **Our edge:** $8B Q1 free cash flow supports aggressive buyback pace

## 5. VMware Integration Synergies
- **What:** Full integration of VMware acquisition (~$69B deal); subscription transition completing
- **When:** Ongoing through 2026

## 6. AI Networking Dominance (Tomahawk 6 / Jericho)
- **What:** Broadcom's Ethernet switches are becoming the default for AI data center networking
- **When:** Ramp through 2026
- **Impact:** +5-10% — networking is a less-noticed but high-margin AI revenue stream
- **Probability:** High — already market leader
- **Market expectation:** Focus is on custom ASICs; networking upside underappreciated
- **Our edge:** Every new AI cluster needs networking — Broadcom wins regardless of which GPU/ASIC is chosen
