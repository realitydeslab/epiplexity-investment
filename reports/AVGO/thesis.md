# AVGO Investment Thesis — Broadcom

## One-Line Thesis
Broadcom is the stealth king of AI infrastructure — custom chips + networking + enterprise software — trading at a reasonable multiple while AI revenue doubles annually.

## The Catalyst
CEO Hock Tan projected **"significantly in excess of $100 billion"** in AI chip revenue by 2027, up from ~$35B annualized run-rate. Backed by signed contracts with Google, Meta, and others, with 4th/5th customers ramping. Q1 FY2026 AI revenue hit $8.4B (+106% YoY). Q2 guided to ~$22B total revenue ($1.5B above consensus).
