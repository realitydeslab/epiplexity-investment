## Assessment: TRANSITIONAL — Growth delayed, not absent

- **LEU segment:** SWU volume +23%, but uranium revenue -54%. Mix shifting to higher-margin SWU — gross profit grew 19% even as revenue dipped.
- **Technical Solutions:** Revenue +11% but gross profit -66% due to HALEU contract cost overruns (undefinitized Phase 2 extension).
- **2026 guidance conservative** — management sandbagging during capex buildout.

## The Real Growth Is In the Backlog

- **$2.3B contingent LEU backlog** → converts as enrichment capacity comes online
- **$900M HALEU contract** → not yet in revenue run rate
- **NNSA sole-source** → entirely new revenue stream
- **SWU pricing rising** → future contracts at higher margins

## Balance Sheet

- **Unrestricted cash: $2.0B** (54% of market cap!)
- No liquidity concerns for multi-year capex

## Verdict
Revenue flat in 2026 as Centrus transitions from broker to vertically integrated enricher. Growth inflection comes 2027-2028 when centrifuge manufacturing scales and backlog converts.
