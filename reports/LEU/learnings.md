## Sector Insights

1. **Nuclear fuel supply chain is the real bottleneck.** Reactors without enriched fuel are expensive paperweights. Centrus sits at the chokepoint.
2. **Russia ban restructured global enrichment permanently.** ~40% of capacity gone from Western markets. Takes a decade to replace.
3. **AI data center power demand is structural.** Hyperscalers signing 15-20 year nuclear PPAs. This is CapEx commitment, not speculation.
4. **US rebuilding strategic industrial capacity.** HALEU enrichment is national security capability. NNSA sole-source signals Centrus = critical infrastructure.
