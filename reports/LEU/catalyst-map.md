# LEU (Centrus Energy) — Catalyst Map 🎯

*Research date: March 7, 2026 | Price: ~$193.72*

---

## Catalyst 1: $900M DOE HALEU Production Award Finalization

- **What:** DOE selected Centrus for a $900M task order to expand HALEU production at Piketon, OH. Award announced Jan 5, 2026 but remains "subject to negotiation."
- **When:** Q1-Q2 2026
- **Impact:** +15-25%. Removes the "subject to negotiation" overhang. Validates HALEU monopoly thesis.
- **Probability:** High (85%). Centrus is the only US entity with demonstrated HALEU enrichment capability.
- **Market expectation:** Consensus assumes it will finalize but timeline uncertainty is priced as a discount.
- **Our edge:** DOE nuclear awards to sole-qualified contractors historically close near original scope.

## Catalyst 2: NNSA Sole-Source Enrichment Award

- **What:** NNSA notified intent to sole-source certain uranium enrichment activities to Centrus — national security enrichment work beyond commercial LEU.
- **When:** Q2-Q3 2026
- **Impact:** +10-15%. Opens entirely new revenue stream with government-grade margins.
- **Probability:** High (80%). Centrus is the only US company with operational centrifuge enrichment capability.
- **Market expectation:** Under-appreciated — most analysts model only commercial LEU and HALEU segments.
- **Our edge:** National security contracts have minimal competition and premium pricing.

## Catalyst 3: Centrifuge Manufacturing Ramp & 12 MT HALEU Target

- **What:** Centrus launched domestic centrifuge manufacturing Dec 2025, building capacity for 12 metric tons HALEU. Reaching "nth-of-a-kind cost" is key profitability milestone.
- **When:** Progress updates throughout 2026; full ramp 2027-2028
- **Impact:** +10-20% on positive milestones; -15% if delays.
- **Probability:** Medium-High (70%).
- **Market expectation:** Consensus models smooth ramp. Execution hiccups will be punished.
- **Our edge:** Centrus has 80+ years institutional knowledge, already produced 1+ MT HALEU.

## Catalyst 4: LEU Pricing Curve Escalation

- **What:** SWU prices rising sharply — Russian enrichment ban + AI data center nuclear demand. $2.3B contingent LEU backlog reprices upward.
- **When:** Ongoing through 2026
- **Impact:** +5-10% per quarter of sustained price increases.
- **Probability:** High (85%). Russia (40% of global enrichment) increasingly sanctioned.
- **Our edge:** Structural supply deficit takes 5-10 years to fill. Centrus is one of few beneficiaries.

## Catalyst 5: Advanced Reactor Fuel Supply Contracts

- **What:** Next-gen reactor developers (X-energy, Kairos, TerraPower) need HALEU fuel. Centrus — only HALEU producer — signs fuel supply contracts.
- **When:** H2 2026+
- **Impact:** +10-20% per major contract.
- **Probability:** Medium (60%).
- **Our edge:** HALEU is the bottleneck. Centrus has monopoly on US HALEU production.

## Catalyst 6: Index/ETF Inclusion & Analyst Coverage Expansion

- **When:** Throughout 2026
- **Impact:** +5-10% from passive flows
- **Probability:** Medium (50%)
