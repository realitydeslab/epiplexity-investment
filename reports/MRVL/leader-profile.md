# MRVL Leader Profile — Matt Murphy, CEO

*Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## CEO: Matt Murphy (Chairman & CEO since 2016)

**Background:** Appointed in 2016 during a period of stagnation and leadership turmoil. Previously held senior roles at Maxim Integrated. Stanford MBA.

**Strategic Vision:** Executed one of the most successful pivots in semiconductor history — from a storage controller company to the "nervous system" of AI data centers. Key acquisitions under his leadership:
- **Cavium** ($6B, 2018) — networking & ARM processors
- **Inphi** ($10B, 2021) — high-speed optical interconnects (PAM4 DSPs)
- **Innovium** (2021) — switching
- **Celestial AI** (2025) — photonic fabric
- **XConn Technologies** ($540M, 2025) — die-to-die interconnect

**Capital Allocation:** Widely regarded as one of the most disciplined allocators in semis. Divested automotive Ethernet to Infineon for $2.5B in late 2025 — used proceeds for debt paydown and AI-focused R&D. Portfolio optimization: "narrow focus to where we have a right to win."

**Execution Track Record:** Excellent. Consistent beat-and-raise on earnings. FY2026 full-year revenue $8.195B (+42% YoY), non-GAAP EPS $2.84 (+81% YoY). Has delivered on every major strategic promise.

**Confidence Level:** Very High. On latest call: "We expect year-over-year revenue growth to accelerate each quarter in fiscal 2027" and "design wins in fiscal 2026 hit an all-time record."
