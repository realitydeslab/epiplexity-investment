## Growth Trajectory — The S-Curve

| Fiscal Year | Revenue | YoY Growth |
|-------------|---------|------------|
| FY2024 | ~$5.5B | -3% (trough) |
| FY2025 | ~$5.77B | +5% (recovery) |

**This is a classic "S-curve" acceleration** — the company went through a trough in FY2024, recovered in FY2025, and is now in full-blown AI-driven hypergrowth.
