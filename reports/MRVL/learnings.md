# MRVL Structured Intelligence — Learnings

*Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## 🧠 What This Tells Us About the Sector

### The "Compute-to-Connectivity Shift" Is Real
The first wave of AI spending (2023-2025) was about GPUs — buying compute. The second wave (2026+) is about **connecting** that compute. Marvell is the primary beneficiary of this shift. This is not hype; it's physics. You can't train a trillion-parameter model without moving data between millions of chips at 1.6 Terabits/second.

### Custom Silicon Is Eating General-Purpose
Hyperscalers are designing their own chips (Amazon Trainium, Google TPU, Microsoft Maia) rather than relying solely on NVIDIA GPUs. Marvell and Broadcom are the "arms dealers" in this war. This trend accelerates as AI workloads become more specialized.

### Photonic Interconnect Is the Next Frontier
The Celestial AI acquisition signals that electronic interconnect is hitting physical limits. Optical I/O between chips (not just between racks) could be a paradigm shift. Marvell is years ahead here. Watch for competitors trying to catch up.

## Adjacent Plays

- **AVGO** — same custom ASIC thesis but with software optionality (VMware)
- **TSM** — fabricates everything, benefits from volume growth at 2nm
- **ANET** — data center networking, complementary to Marvell's optics
- **COHR** — optical components supplier, rides the same 1.6T wave
- **CLS** (Celestica) — AI server manufacturing, Marvell chip end-customer

## Contrarian Angle: What's the Market Missing?

1. **Celestial AI revenue is not in most models.** Analysts haven't fully incorporated the $500M→$1B CPO trajectory. This is free upside.
2. **The PEG ratio is 0.66.** For a 30%+ grower with expanding margins, this is genuinely undervalued. The market is still punishing MRVL for the 2025 valuation reset.
3. **Five hyperscaler DCI wins.** Supplying ALL major cloud providers with DCI modules is unprecedented diversification. Reduces single-customer risk dramatically.
4. **The automotive divestiture was genius.** $2.5B from Infineon, redeployed into AI. This kind of capital allocation discipline is rare.
