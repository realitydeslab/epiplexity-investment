## Margin Trajectory — CONCERNING
Q2 FY2026 showed **margin compression** despite massive revenue growth.
- Revenue growing 122.8% but EPS only 16.9% → margin compression confirmed
- At $18.75B market cap on $50B+ annualized revenue, P/S ratio is ~0.37x — absurdly cheap IF margins stabilize

## Verdict
**Growth is ACCELERATING sharply.** Revenue trajectory is exceptional. The concern is whether growth comes at the expense of profitability. If margins stabilize, this is wildly undervalued. If margins continue compressing, it's a revenue trap.
