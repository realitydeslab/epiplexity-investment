## Risk Management

- **Stop-loss:** $130 (-17%) — below the recent base and psychological support
- **Position sizing:** 3-5% of portfolio max — high-conviction but extreme valuation risk
- **What kills the trade:**
  1. Growth deceleration below 50% YoY for two consecutive quarters
  2. US commercial growth dropping below 80% (would suggest AIP stalling)
  3. Major government contract loss or political reversal
  4. Broad tech multiple compression (macro risk)
- **Correlation risk:** Highly correlated with AI/tech sentiment. If NVDA drops hard, PLTR follows.
