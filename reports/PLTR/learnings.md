## Contrarian Angle

**What the market might be missing:**

**Bull contrarian:** International government is the undiscovered country. Everyone focuses on US commercial and US gov. But UK MoD, NATO expansion, Five Eyes partnerships could be a $2B+ revenue stream by 2028 that nobody is modeling.

**Bear contrarian:** The Rule of 40 at 127% is partly a function of deferred SBC costs. GAAP operating margin (32% FY2025) vs adjusted (50%) shows a large gap driven by stock-based comp. As SBC normalizes post-IPO grant cycles, the gap should narrow — but it also means real profitability is lower than headlines suggest.

**Valuation contrarian:** At 52x FY2026 revenue, even with 60%+ growth, you're pricing in sustained 40%+ growth through 2030. Any hiccup and the stock drops 40% fast. The $400M insider selling in 2 weeks suggests the people who know the company best think it's at least fairly valued.
