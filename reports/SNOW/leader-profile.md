# SNOW — Leader Profile

*As of March 7, 2026*

## CEO: Sridhar Ramaswamy

- **Background:** Former SVP of Ads & Commerce at Google; joined Snowflake as CEO in Feb 2024 after Frank Slootman's retirement
- **Prior:** Founded Neeva (AI-powered search engine), acquired by Snowflake in 2023
- **Strengths:** Deep AI/ML expertise from Google + Neeva; understands enterprise software at scale
- **Assessment:** Strong fit for AI-era Snowflake. His Google background gives credibility with enterprise customers and AI partnerships (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Cloud). Under his leadership, Snowflake shipped 430+ new capabilities in FY2026 and signed its largest-ever deal ($400M+).

## Insider Activity

- **CEO RSU Grant:** Ramaswamy awarded 101,402 RSUs tied to FY2026 performance goals being met (filed Feb 26, 2026). Vesting over 4 years — signals confidence in multi-year trajectory.
- **No significant insider selling** reported around Q4 earnings
- **Key signal:** Performance-based RSUs vesting = board confirmed FY2026 goals were hit

## Key Hires/Departures

- **178 Observe employees** integrated in Q1 FY2027 (observability talent)
- **~200 person RIF** in Q4 FY2026 — restructuring to streamline; net adds were 37 in Q4
- Christian Kleinerman (EVP Product) remains in place — strong product leadership continuity
- Brian Robbins (CFO) — steady hand on financials

## Public Statements (Q4 Call)

- "AI is not a feature — it's becoming the core of how customers use Snowflake"
- Highlighted Cortex Code cutting internal development cycles
- Emphasized multi-model strategy as competitive advantage
- Guided FY2027 product revenue $5.66B (+27%), non-GAAP operating margin 12.5%
- Q1 FY2027 guidance: product revenue $1.262-1.267B (+27%)

## Verdict

Ramaswamy is the right leader for AI-era Snowflake. He has the technical chops (Google AI, Neeva) and the enterprise relationships. The performance RSU grant confirms the board sees him delivering. No red flags on insider selling. The small RIF is a positive — cutting costs while growing 30%.
