# SNOW — Catalyst Map (12-Month Horizon)

*As of March 7, 2026*

## 1. FY2027 Revenue Acceleration from AI Workloads
- **What:** Snowflake guided FY2027 product revenue of $5.66B (+27% YoY). AI workloads (Cortex AI, Snowflake Intelligence) are the fastest-growing segment.
- **When:** Quarterly earnings — Q1 FY2027 (late May 2026), Q2 (Aug), Q3 (Nov), Q4 (Feb 2027)
- **Impact:** Each 2-3% beat could drive 5-10% stock moves. Full-year beat to ~$6B would rerate significantly.
- **Probability:** High — beaten consensus EPS 6 consecutive quarters.
- **Market expectation:** 27% growth as guided.
- **Our edge:** AI workload mix accelerating faster than base business. $400M record deal + 7 nine-figure contracts in Q4 signal enterprise AI inflection. RPO $9.77B (+42% YoY) = locked-in future revenue.

## 2. Observe Acquisition Integration (Observability)
- **What:** Acquired Observe (AI-powered observability) — 178 employees added Q1. Opens $50B IT ops TAM.
- **When:** Integration H1 2026, revenue ramp H2 2026
- **Impact:** +5-8% on product revenue if integration succeeds; narrative shift to "platform"
- **Probability:** Medium-High
- **Market expectation:** Modest contribution priced in
- **Our edge:** Unified data platform + observability creates moat Datadog/Splunk can't replicate.

## 3. AI Model Partnerships (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google Cloud)
- **What:** Native access to Claude, GPT, Gemini within Snowflake. OpenAI expanded to $200M deal.
- **When:** Enterprise adoption ramps through 2026
- **Impact:** +10-15% AI workload consumption; key differentiator vs. Databricks
- **Probability:** High
- **Our edge:** Multi-model "Switzerland" approach — customers avoid vendor lock-in.

## 4. Cortex Code & Snowflake Intelligence GA Adoption
- **What:** 430+ new capabilities in FY2026 including Cortex Code and Snowflake Intelligence (natural language querying)
- **When:** Adoption curves through 2026
- **Impact:** Consumption amplifiers — every query = more compute credits
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Our edge:** These are consumption multipliers, not just features.

## 5. Net Revenue Retention Re-Acceleration
- **What:** NRR at 125% in Q4. AI workloads could push back toward 130%+.
- **When:** Tracked quarterly
- **Impact:** NRR >130% = durable hypergrowth signal; stock rerates to 20x+ forward revenue
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Our edge:** Seven nine-figure expansion contracts suggest large customers spending aggressively.

## 6. Legal Overhang Resolution
- **What:** Multiple securities class action lawsuits over past disclosures
- **When:** Settlement/dismissal possible in 2026
- **Impact:** Resolution removes 5-10% risk discount
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Our edge:** Standard post-IPO fare; most settle for immaterial amounts.

## 7. Macro: Rate Cuts Favor Growth Stocks
- **What:** Fed rate cuts in 2026 benefit high-growth SaaS names
- **When:** Fed meetings throughout 2026
- **Impact:** 15-25% sector-wide multiple expansion possible
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Our edge:** SNOW's strong FCF ($782M adjusted, +61% YoY) makes it less rate-sensitive than unprofitable growth.

## 8. SAP Partnership Expansion
- **What:** Expanded SAP partnership announced in Q4 — integrating Snowflake with SAP data ecosystems
- **When:** Through 2026
- **Impact:** Opens massive enterprise SAP customer base to Snowflake
- **Probability:** High
- **Our edge:** SAP enterprises are the largest data producers — this is a huge greenfield.
