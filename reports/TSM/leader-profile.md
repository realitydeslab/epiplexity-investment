# TSM (TSMC) — Leader Profile 👤

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Dr. C.C. Wei — Chairman & CEO

- **Role:** Chairman and CEO (dual role since June 2024, CEO since 2018)
- **Background:** Ph.D. Electrical Engineering, Yale. 26+ years at TSMC. Previously at Chartered Semiconductor, ST Microelectronics, Texas Instruments.
- **Track record:** Under Wei's leadership as CEO (2018-present), TSMC revenue grew from ~$35B to $120B+ (2025). Hit the $100B target 5 years ahead of schedule.

### Execution Assessment

**Strengths:**
- Navigated the geopolitical minefield of U.S.-China tech war while growing revenues 30%+ YoY
- Successfully launched N2 GAA technology on schedule — the most complex architectural transition in a decade
- Made Arizona profitable — a feat many doubted possible given higher U.S. costs
- Managed CoWoS bottleneck by committing $50B+ capex without hesitation
- Quote from earnings call: "I'm also very nervous" about AI demand — shows healthy caution, not blind optimism

**Concerns:**
- First person to hold dual Chairman/CEO role at TSMC — concentration of power
- Succession planning unclear for post-Wei era
- Age factor — Wei is in his late 60s

### Insider Activity

- TSMC insiders (including Wei) have NOT been significant sellers — no large insider sales flagged in recent quarters
- As a Taiwan-listed company, insider trading patterns are less transparent than U.S. peers
- No unusual selling around the Q4 2025 earnings beat — bullish signal

### Key Leadership Team

- **Wendell Huang** — CFO. Delivered strong guidance at Q4 earnings. Clear communicator on capex strategy.
- **Y.P. Chyn** — EVP & Co-COO. Operations execution.
- Leadership team is deep and experienced — no key-person departures in 2025-2026

### Verdict

C.C. Wei is arguably the most important CEO in the semiconductor industry right now. His track record of execution is exceptional — N2 on time, Arizona profitable, 8 consecutive earnings beats. He's the right leader for this moment. **Rating: A+**
