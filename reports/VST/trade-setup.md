## Risk Management

### Stop-Loss
- **Hard stop:** $140 (-16%) — below 200 DMA and major technical support
- **Mental stop:** Cogentrix deal falls through or major nuclear operational issue

### Position Sizing
- **Recommended:** 3-5% of portfolio
- **Rationale:** High conviction on structural AI power demand, but stock has already 7x'd. Not a "bet the farm" entry. Better risk/reward than CEG at current valuation.

### What Kills the Trade?
1. **AI demand plateau** — if hyperscalers slow capex, the "nuclear for AI" premium evaporates
2. **Nuclear incident** (anywhere globally) — regulatory crackdown would crater all nuclear stocks
3. **Management leverage blowout** — if Cogentrix + other deals push leverage above 3.5x
4. **Power market deregulation reversal** — political risk in Texas/PJM

### Correlation Risk
- Highly correlated to: CEG, OKLO, LEU, other nuclear/energy names
- Correlated to: AI infrastructure spending (NVDA, cloud capex)
- Inverse correlation to: Natural gas price crashes (mixed — hurts gas fleet, helps retail margins)
