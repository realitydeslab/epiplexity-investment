# VST Leader Profile — Vistra Energy

*Research date: March 7, 2026*

## CEO: James "Jim" Burke

- **Role:** President & CEO since 2022
- **Background:** TXU/EFH veteran — lived through the $45B LBO disaster and $33B bankruptcy. This scarred him in the right ways.
- **Philosophy:** "Capital Allocation" mantra — balance shareholder returns with strategic investment. Avoids reckless expansionism.
- **Track record:** Under Burke, Vistra has executed flawlessly:
  - Energy Harbor acquisition (2024) — added nuclear fleet
  - Lotus Infrastructure acquisition (completed late 2025) — 2,600 MW gas
  - Cogentrix acquisition ($4.7B, announced Jan 2026) — 5,500 MW gas
  - AWS PPA (1,200 MW Comanche Peak nuclear)
  - Meta PPA (2,176 MW nuclear + 433 MW uprates)
  - ~30% share count reduction since 2021

**Assessment:** Burke is exactly the right CEO for this moment. Not a visionary — a disciplined operator who buys assets cheap and runs them well. He's turning Vistra from a legacy utility into an AI infrastructure play through smart acquisitions and premium nuclear contracts.
