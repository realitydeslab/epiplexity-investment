# ISRG Catalyst Map — Intuitive Surgical

*Research Date: March 7, 2026 | Current Price: ~$490*

## 1. da Vinci 5 Cardiac Surgery Rollout (Q1-Q4 2026)

- **What:** FDA cleared da Vinci 5 for 9 cardiac procedures in Jan 2026. Reopens a market Intuitive largely abandoned 15+ years ago due to technical limitations on older systems.
- **When:** Measured rollout throughout 2026; meaningful revenue contribution likely H2 2026 into 2027.
- **Impact:** ~17,000 cardiac procedures were done on legacy dV Si/Xi in 2025. dV5's advanced capabilities (force feedback, 3D visualization upgrades) could unlock significant TAM expansion. Potential +3-5% to procedure growth over 12 months.
- **Probability:** High (clearance already obtained)
- **Market Expectation:** Most analysts model "measured" rollout with limited 2026 contribution.
- **Our Edge:** The cardiac surgery market is massive (~$15B+ globally). Even modest penetration on dV5 = meaningful upside. 17K procedures on inferior legacy systems proves demand exists — dV5 removes the capability bottleneck.

## 2. Q1 2026 Earnings (Late April 2026)

- **What:** First full quarter with dV5 cardiac clearance, continued dV5 ramp, European distribution integration impact.
- **When:** Late April 2026
- **Impact:** Beat expectations = +5-8% move. Miss = -8-12%.
- **Probability:** High probability of beat (8+ consecutive quarters of beats)
- **Market Expectation:** ~$2.65-2.70 EPS, ~$2.95B revenue
- **Our Edge:** dV5 placements accelerating (303 in Q4 vs 174 in Q3), instrument attach rate for dV5 may surprise.

## 3. European Distribution In-Housing (2026)

- **What:** Acquired distributor operations in Italy, Spain, Portugal, Malta, San Marino for ~€319M. Brings distribution in-house.
- **When:** Integration throughout 2026; margin accretion expected H2 2026
- **Impact:** +2-3% revenue uplift from capturing distributor margin. Near-term margin drag, then long-term expansion.
- **Probability:** High (deal closed)
- **Our Edge:** Every prior in-housing (Japan, Korea) has been margin accretive within 12-18 months. Playbook is proven.

## 4. My Intuitive+ Digital Subscription Launch (2026)

- **What:** Paid digital subscription platform for surgeons — analytics, training, performance data.
- **When:** Rollout through 2026
- **Impact:** Creates new SaaS-like revenue stream. Could add $100-200M ARR at scale. Shifts narrative toward recurring software revenue.
- **Probability:** Medium-High
- **Our Edge:** 11,100+ installed systems = massive built-in user base. Software revenue commands higher multiples than hardware.

## 5. Ion Endoluminal Platform Inflection (2026)

- **What:** Ion procedures grew 44% YoY in Q4 2025. Platform approaching 1,000 installed systems.
- **When:** Ongoing through 2026
- **Impact:** Ion could become a $1B+ revenue contributor. 44% growth rate suggests accelerating adoption.
- **Probability:** High
- **Our Edge:** Ion's lung biopsy market is early innings. If bronchoscopy screening guidelines expand (USPSTF review pending), TAM explodes.

## 6. Macro: Interest Rate Cuts (2026)

- **What:** Rate cuts make capital equipment purchases more attractive for hospitals. dV5 ASP is ~$2.5M per system.
- **When:** Dependent on Fed policy, potentially H2 2026
- **Impact:** Lower rates = accelerated hospital capex = more placements.
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Our Edge:** Hospital CFOs have been delaying capital decisions. Rate-cut signal could unleash pent-up demand.
