## Moat Assessment

1. **Installed base lock-in:** 11,100 systems, enormous switching costs
2. **Training:** Surgeons spend 100+ hours credentialing on da Vinci
3. **Clinical evidence:** 20+ years of outcomes data
4. **Instrument ecosystem:** $800-2,000 per procedure in consumables
5. **Regulatory head start:** 60+ cleared procedure types

## Pair Trade Opportunities

- Long ISRG / Short MDT (robotics leader vs struggling competitor)
- Long ISRG + Long HCA (both benefit from hospital capex recovery)

## Competitive Timeline

- **2026:** Hugo may gain clearances but won't reach meaningful scale
- **2027-28:** J&J Ottava could enter — first real test
- **Bottom line:** Competitive position unassailable in 2026
