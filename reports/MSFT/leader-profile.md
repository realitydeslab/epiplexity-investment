# MSFT Leader Profile — March 2026

## Satya Nadella — Chairman & CEO

**Tenure:** CEO since Feb 2014 (12 years). Chairman since June 2021.

**Track record:** Arguably the most successful CEO transition in tech history. Transformed Microsoft from stagnant Windows dependency into a $3T cloud + AI juggernaut. Revenue: ~$86B (FY2014) → ~$305B TTM (3.5x).

**AI positioning:** Made OpenAI partnership centerpiece of strategy. Q2 FY2026 quote: "We are only at the beginning phases of AI diffusion and already Microsoft has built an AI business that is larger than some of our biggest franchises."

**Key strength:** Platform thinking. Integrates AI across entire stack (Azure, M365, Dynamics, GitHub, LinkedIn) — widest AI surface area of any company.

**Risk:** OpenAI dependency. If relationship fractures, pivot cost is real (though MSFT has deep internal AI talent).

## Amy Hood — EVP & CFO

**Tenure:** Since 2013. One of tech's most respected CFOs.

**Capital allocation:** Returned $12.7B to shareholders in Q2 FY2026 (+32% YoY) while scaling capex massively. Conservative guidance → consistent beats.

## Mustafa Suleyman — CEO of Microsoft AI

**Joined:** March 2024 from Inflection AI. Leads consumer AI/Copilot products. DeepMind co-founder — significant talent acquisition.
