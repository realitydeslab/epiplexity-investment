# CRM (Salesforce) — Catalyst Map

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## 1. Agentforce Monetization Inflection (Q1–Q4 FY27)

- **What:** Agentforce ARR hit $800M (+169% YoY) in Q4 FY26. Management guiding for "organic revenue reacceleration in 2H FY27" driven by Agentforce adoption ramping beyond early adopters.
- **When:** Gradual throughout FY27 (Feb 2026–Jan 2027), with inflection expected H2 (Aug 2026+)
- **Impact:** If Agentforce reaches $2B+ ARR by end of FY27, could add 2-3pts to organic growth, driving stock to $250+. Potential +25-30% from current levels.
- **Probability:** Medium-High. $800M→$2B requires ~150% growth, in line with current trajectory.
- **Market expectation:** Consensus expects ~8% organic growth. Market is skeptical Agentforce translates to consolidated revenue acceleration.
- **Our edge:** The $72B RPO (+14%) and $35B CRPO (+16%) suggest bookings are running ahead of recognized revenue. Agentforce consumption model means revenue lags adoption.

## 2. Informatica Integration Synergies (Q1–Q2 FY27)

- **What:** Informatica acquisition contributing ~$1.6B/year (~$400M/quarter). Cross-sell into Data 360 + Agentforce stack. Agentforce + Data 360 ARR already $2.9B (+200% YoY).
- **When:** Full integration by mid-2026
- **Impact:** +5-10% if data management becomes the "on-ramp" to Agentforce. Could boost net revenue retention.
- **Probability:** High. Salesforce has successfully integrated acquisitions before (Slack, Tableau, MuleSoft).
- **Market expectation:** Market largely discounts Informatica as just inorganic padding.

## 3. $50B Share Buyback Execution (Ongoing through FY27-29)

- **What:** Largest software buyback in history. Already returned $14.3B in FY26 ($12.7B buybacks). At current ~$190B market cap, $50B authorization = ~26% of float over time.
- **When:** Ongoing, likely front-loaded given stock is near 52-week lows
- **Impact:** Mechanical EPS accretion of 5-8% annually from buybacks alone
- **Probability:** High. Already executing aggressively.

## 4. Q1 FY27 Earnings (Late May 2026)

- **What:** First quarter with full Informatica contribution + Agentforce ramp. Guidance: $11.03-11.08B revenue.
- **When:** ~May 27-28, 2026
- **Impact:** Beat + raised guidance could trigger 10-15% move. Miss on organic growth could push stock to $170s.
- **Probability:** Medium-High for beat (Salesforce has consistently beaten estimates)

## 5. Enterprise AI Spending Acceleration (H2 2026)

- **What:** Enterprise AI budgets shifting from experimental to production. Salesforce positioned as incumbent CRM + AI agent platform.
- **When:** H2 2026 as budget cycles reset
- **Impact:** Could drive organic growth to 12-15% if AI becomes a must-have line item
- **Probability:** Medium. Macro uncertainty could delay enterprise spending.

## 6. Multiple Expansion from AI Narrative

- **What:** If Agentforce growth sustains, Salesforce could re-rate from "mature SaaS" (25-30x P/E) to "AI platform" (35-45x P/E)
- **When:** Dependent on 2-3 consecutive quarters of organic acceleration
- **Impact:** Multiple expansion from 26x to 35x = ~35% upside on current earnings
- **Probability:** Low-Medium. Requires clear evidence of durable acceleration.

## 7. Macro: Fed Rate Cuts

- **What:** Lower rates benefit high-duration growth stocks. Enterprise software spending correlates with easing cycles.
- **When:** Uncertain, market pricing multiple cuts in 2026
- **Impact:** 5-10% tailwind from valuation expansion
- **Probability:** Medium
