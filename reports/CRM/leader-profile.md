# CRM (Salesforce) — Leader Profile

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Marc Benioff — Chair & CEO

- **Tenure:** Founded Salesforce in 1999, CEO since inception. 27 years at the helm.
- **Track record:** Built Salesforce from zero to $41.5B revenue. Invented the SaaS model. Successfully integrated Slack, Tableau, MuleSoft, ExactTarget, and now Informatica.
- **AI pivot credibility:** Fully embraced the "Agentic Enterprise" narrative. 2.4B agentic work units delivered; 19T tokens processed. Reorganized all product lines under agentic branding.
- **Execution risk:** Benioff's visionary tendencies sometimes outrun execution (Metaverse/Web3 flirtation in 2022, Slack integration took longer than expected). The $800M ARR suggests this pivot has substance.
