# CRM (Salesforce) — Competitive Map

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Direct Competitors

### Microsoft (MSFT) — Copilot + Dynamics 365
- **Threat:** HIGH. 400M+ Office seats, bundling AI agents with existing licenses.
- **CRM advantage:** Purpose-built CRM with 25+ years of workflow data, deeper automation.

### ServiceNow (NOW) — AI Agents for ITSM
- **Threat:** MEDIUM. Expanding from IT into customer workflows.
- **CRM advantage:** Salesforce dominates front-office; ServiceNow stronger in ITSM.

### Oracle (ORCL) — Fusion Cloud + AI
- **Threat:** MEDIUM. Strong in back-office/ERP.

### HubSpot (HUBS) — SMB CRM
- **Threat:** LOW. Different market segment.

## Same-Catalyst Beneficiaries

If enterprise AI agent spending accelerates: ServiceNow (NOW), Workday (WDAY), MongoDB (MDB), Datadog (DDOG)

## Who Loses if CRM Wins

Legacy on-prem CRM, point solution vendors, BPO/outsourcing (Concentrix, TTEC, Teleperformance)

## Pair Trades

- Long CRM / Short legacy outsourcers (CNXC, TTEC)
- Long CRM / Short HUBS

## Moat: STRONG — CRM data + workflow lock-in. Risk: MSFT bundling makes Agentforce premium pricing harder to justify.
