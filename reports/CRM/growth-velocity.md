# CRM (Salesforce) — Growth Velocity

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Revenue Growth (FY26 Quarters)

| Quarter | Revenue | YoY Growth | CC Growth |
|---------|---------|------------|-----------|
| Q1 FY26 | ~$9.6B | ~8% | ~8% |
| Q2 FY26 | ~$9.9B | ~8% | ~8% |
| Q3 FY26 | ~$10.3B | ~8% | ~8% |
| Q4 FY26 | $11.2B | 12% | 10% |

**Full Year FY26:** $41.5B (+10% YoY, +9% CC)

## Trend: STABLE (not yet accelerating organically)

- Q4 12% YoY includes ~$399M Informatica → organic ~8-9% CC
- Organic growth roughly flat across all four quarters
- **Bookings tell a different story:** CRPO +16% YoY, total RPO +14% — bookings outpacing revenue

## Agentforce (the growth engine)

- Agentforce ARR: $800M (+169% YoY)
- Agentforce + Data 360 ARR: $2.9B (+200% YoY)
- 2.4B agentic work units delivered
- Question: when does this move the needle on a $41.5B base?

## Profitability

| Metric | FY26 | Trend |
|--------|------|-------|
| Non-GAAP Op Margin | 34.1% | ↑ |
| OCF | $15.0B (+15%) | ↑ |
| FCF | $14.4B (+16%) | ↑ |
| Adjusted EPS | $12.52 (+23%) | ↑ |

FCF margin ~35% is elite. Profitability steadily improving.

## FY27 Guidance

- Revenue: $45.8-46.2B (~11% growth, ~3pts Informatica → organic ~8%)
- Management promises "organic reacceleration in 2H FY27"

## Verdict

Growth is stable but not inflecting. Leading indicators (RPO, Agentforce ARR) are bullish, but consolidated revenue hasn't caught up. **Show-me story.**
