# CRM (Salesforce) — Strategic Learnings

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

## Sector Intelligence

1. **"Agentic AI" is the new enterprise narrative.** Salesforce's rebrand signals the market moving from chatbots → autonomous agents. This theme drives 2026-2027 software spending.

2. **Consumption-based AI revenue lags adoption.** $800M ARR with 2.4B work units = usage growing faster than revenue. This applies to ALL enterprise AI — bookings > revenue for now.

3. **"Mature SaaS" discount is real.** 8% organic growth gets 26x P/E. Palantir at 30%+ gets 100x+. Large-cap SaaS punished for not showing AI inflection.

## Adjacent Plays

- **MongoDB (MDB):** Data infrastructure for AI agent apps
- **Datadog (DDOG):** Observability for AI workloads
- **Twilio (TWLO):** Communication infrastructure for AI agents

## Contrarian Angle

**The RPO signal.** CRPO growing 16% vs. revenue at 10% means $5B+ in contracted revenue not yet recognized. If RPO growth sustains, organic revenue MUST accelerate — just a timing question (2-4 quarters).

**The buyback floor.** $50B at $190B market cap = 5-8% annual share retirement. Mechanical EPS floor even if revenue stalls.
