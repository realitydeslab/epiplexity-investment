# Alibaba Group (9988.HK / BABA) — Vision Report

**Date:** 2026-03-06
**Analyst:** Biber (Epiplexity Research)

## Latest Developments

### Q3 FY2026 Earnings (reported ~March 5, 2026)
- **Revenue:** Consensus ~$40.95B; previous Q showed 5% YoY growth (15% ex-disposals)
- **Q2 FY2026 (Nov 2025):** Revenue up 5% YoY; Cloud revenue grew 13%; EPS $2.95 beat estimate of $2.66
- Stock soared 8%+ on Q2 beat; Q3 results just reported March 5

### AI & Cloud Strategy
- **Qwen 3.5** AI model launched — multimodal with agentic capabilities (food delivery, travel, payments via chat)
- CEO Eddie Wu: "doesn't see much of an issue with AI bubble" — investing "aggressively"
- **Tens of billions of dollars** committed to data center and AI infrastructure
- Cloud revenue maintaining ~13% growth trajectory
- AI demand outpacing global chip supply per management

### E-Commerce & Retail
- Instant commerce integration into Taobao app drove **20% rise in daily active users**
- Revenue estimates: FY26 RMB 1,036B; FY27 RMB 1,154B
- China Commerce remains revenue engine; consumer demand recovery is key variable

### Regulatory & Geopolitical
- U.S. DoD briefly placed Alibaba on military-linked companies list (damaged sentiment)
- Ongoing China regulatory uncertainty remains overhang
- Stock down ~1.69% YTD despite 10% gain in 2025

## Strategic Direction
Alibaba is pivoting from pure e-commerce to **AI-first cloud infrastructure**. Aggressive AI capex, Qwen development, and cloud margin focus signal management sees the future in enterprise AI services. Integration of AI agents into consumer apps bridges the commerce-AI gap.
