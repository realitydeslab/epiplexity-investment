# Leader Profile: Alibaba Group (9988.HK)

*Research Date: March 7, 2026*

---

## CEO: Eddie Wu Yongming (吴泳铭)

- **Role:** CEO since September 2023 (replaced Daniel Zhang)
- **Background:** Alibaba co-founder (#1 employee), former CTO, built Alipay. Deep technical roots.
- **Assessment:** RIGHT PERSON for this phase. Technical founder leading an AI pivot > professional manager.

### Recent Actions (Bullish Signals)
- **March 5, 2026:** Personally formed and leads a new AI task force to "jointly coordinate group-wide resources to accelerate foundational model development." This is CEO-level prioritization — not delegation.
- Stated: "In technology, standing still means falling behind. Advancing foundation models is a core strategic priority for our future."
- Committed to open-source strategy: "uphold our open-source model strategy"
- Overseeing RMB380B ($52B) 3-year AI investment — the largest in Chinese tech

### Leadership Risk: Qwen Lead Departure 🚨
- **March 4, 2026:** Lin Junyang, technical lead for Qwen foundational models, resigned unexpectedly. Announced on X.
- Sent "shock waves through the global open-source AI community" (SCMP)
- Wu's immediate response (task force formation next day) shows decisive crisis management
- Task force now includes: Eddie Wu, Zhou Jingren (Alibaba Cloud CTO), Wu Zeming (food delivery head)
- **Risk assessment:** Near-term disruption to Qwen development velocity. Medium-term: manageable if talent pipeline fills the gap.

## Key Hires/Structure Changes
- Zhou Jingren elevated as CTO of Alibaba Cloud — signal of cloud/AI priority
- Task force structure bypasses normal hierarchy — faster execution

## Insider Activity
- **Institutional buying:** Dimensional Fund Advisors +8.9% stake (2M+ shares, ~$363M). Norges Bank, Alkeon, Artisan, ARK all building positions.
- **SPX Gestao:** +6,600% increase in position (Q3)
- **National Bank of Canada:** Added 475K shares
- **Institutional ownership:** 13.47% — relatively low for a company this size, suggesting room for more institutional accumulation
- **Concern:** Appaloosa (Tepper) cut stake — notable bear signal from a respected fund

## Public Statements on Near-Term Outlook
- Eddie Wu (Nov 2025): "We have entered into an investment phase to build long-term strategic value in AI technologies and infrastructure"
- CFO Toby Xu: "We are re-investing our profits and free cash flow for the future while near-term profitability is expected to fluctuate"
- Translation: Margins will be pressured but this is intentional capex for AI infrastructure

## Verdict
Eddie Wu is the right leader for this moment — technical co-founder leading an AI transformation with urgency and capital. The Qwen lead departure is a real risk but the immediate task force response is reassuring. Institutional smart money is accumulating.
