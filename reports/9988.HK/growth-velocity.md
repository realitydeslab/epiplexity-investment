# Growth Velocity: Alibaba Group (9988.HK)

*Research Date: March 7, 2026*

---

## Revenue Growth: ACCELERATING (on a like-for-like basis)

### Quarterly Revenue Trajectory

| Quarter | Revenue (RMB B) | YoY Growth | Like-for-Like Growth* |
|---------|-----------------|------------|----------------------|
| Q4 FY2025 (Mar 2025) | ~RMB222B | +7% | +12% |
| Q1 FY2026 (Jun 2025) | ~RMB243B | +5% | +11% |
| Q2 FY2026 (Sep 2025) | RMB247.8B | +5% | **+15%** |
| Q3 FY2026 (Dec 2025) | TBD (Mar 19) | Est. +8-10% | Est. +14-16% |

*Excluding disposed businesses (Sun Art, Intime)

**Key insight:** Headline 5% growth is misleading. On a like-for-like basis, growth is **accelerating** — from 11% to 15%. The disposals of underperforming assets are making the core business look slow.

## Profitability: DECLINING (Intentionally)

| Metric | Q2 FY2026 | YoY Change |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| GAAP Net Income | RMB20.6B | **-53%** |
| Adjusted EBITDA | Up 36% | Positive |
| Operating Income | RMB5.4B | Sharp decline |
| Operating Cash Flow | RMB10.1B | Decline |

**Why:** RMB120B ($16.5B) deployed in capex over 4 quarters for AI/cloud infrastructure. This is Amazon 2015-era investment spending.

## Guidance vs Actual History
- Management consistently guided conservatively then beat on revenue
- Explicitly guided for "near-term profitability fluctuation" — setting low bar
- RMB380B 3-year AI commitment = significant continued capex

## Unit Economics
- Take rates expanding via technology fees and GenAI marketing tools
- Quick commerce unit economics "significantly improved" but still loss-making
- Cloud margins compressed by AI infrastructure buildout but AI revenue mix improving

## Path to Profitability Inflection
- Cloud: AI workloads carry higher margins than traditional cloud. As AI mix increases (now >30% of cloud), blended margins should improve by H2 2026.
- E-commerce: Quick commerce approaching breakeven. Same-day delivery war with JD/Meituan may ease.
- Expected: Profitability trough in FY2026, recovery in FY2027 as AI infrastructure investment matures.

## Verdict: Growth is ACCELERATING where it matters

The headline numbers hide a powerful story. Core business (ex-disposals) growing 15%, cloud at 34%, AI at triple digits. Profitability is deliberately sacrificed for AI infrastructure — the right trade-off. This is a growth stock hiding inside value stock packaging.
