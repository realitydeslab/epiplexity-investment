### Stop-Loss
- **Hard stop:** $110 (-16%) — below major support

- **Recommended:** 3-5% of portfolio
- Higher conviction after March 19 earnings confirmation
- China single-stock risk warrants smaller position than US equivalents

### What Kills the Trade?
1. Cloud growth decelerates below 25% (signals AI tailwind fading)
2. Qwen development stalls and competitors leap ahead
3. Major regulatory action (new antitrust, data security)
4. US-China tensions → ADR delisting risk materializes
5. Consumer spending collapses despite stimulus

### Correlation Risk
- Correlated with: China macro (CSI 300, HSI), global AI trade, US-China relations
- If China macro tanks AND AI hype cools simultaneously, double whammy
- Partially hedged by: Buyback floor, valuation discount already baked in
