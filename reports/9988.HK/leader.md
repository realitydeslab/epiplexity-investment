# Alibaba Group (9988.HK / BABA) — Leader Report

**Date:** 2026-03-06
**Analyst:** Biber (Epiplexity Research)

## CEO: Eddie Wu (吴泳铭)

### Background
- Took over as CEO September 2023 during major restructuring
- Co-founder of Alibaba (one of the original 18 founders)
- Previously CEO of Taobao and Tmall Group; CTO of Ant Group
- Deep technical background — one of Alibaba's earliest engineers

### Leadership Style & Track Record
- **Aggressive on AI:** Publicly stated he doesn't see "much of an issue" with AI bubble (Nov 2025)
- Committed to investing "aggressively" in AI infrastructure
- Initiated restructuring that split Alibaba into six business groups
- Divesting non-core assets to focus on cloud + commerce
- Under his leadership: Cloud growth accelerated, Qwen AI model became competitive

### Strategic Priorities (2025-2026)
1. AI-first transformation — massive capex into data centers and AI
2. Cloud margin expansion — shifting from growth to profitability
3. Consumer engagement — instant commerce, DAU growth
4. Capital returns — buyback program and dividend policy

### Insider Activity
- Significant share buybacks ongoing (reducing float)
- Jack Ma gradually reducing involvement; Eddie Wu is operational leader
- No notable insider selling red flags

### Assessment
Eddie Wu is a credible, technically-grounded CEO with founder DNA. Bold AI bets while focusing on margin discipline is a positive signal. Risk is over-investment without near-term returns, but cloud acceleration suggests operational competence.

**Leadership Grade: B+**
