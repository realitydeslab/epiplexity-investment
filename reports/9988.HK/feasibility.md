## Investment Thesis
**Value play with AI optionality.** At ~20x P/E (below sector avg ~21.5x), you get China's dominant cloud + commerce platform at a discount from regulatory/geopolitical fear. AI pivot under Eddie Wu is credible, cloud growth is real, balance sheet is fortress-strong.

**Verdict: Attractive risk/reward. Accumulate on weakness.**
