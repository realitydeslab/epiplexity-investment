# AMZN Leader Profile 👤

*Amazon.com, Inc. | Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## CEO: Andy Jassy

- **Role:** President and CEO (since July 2021, previously CEO of AWS from its founding)
- **Background:** Built AWS from scratch into a $100B+ business. Harvard MBA. 27+ years at Amazon
- **Strengths:** Deep technical understanding of cloud/AI; operational discipline; led Amazon's cost restructuring in 2023-2025 that took operating margins from 2.4% to 11.2%
- **Concerns:** E-commerce innovation has slowed under his tenure; some question whether an AWS-focused CEO can drive retail innovation
- **Verdict:** RIGHT person for the AI moment. AWS is THE catalyst, and Jassy knows cloud better than anyone alive

- **Matthew Garman** — CEO of AWS (since June 2024, replacing Adam Selipsky). Former VP of Sales/Marketing at AWS. Indicates Amazon wants a more commercially aggressive AWS

- **Jeff Bezos** — Executive Chairman. Still holds ~9% of shares (~$200B+). No significant recent sales

- No major executive departures in 2025-2026

## Assessment

**Rating: 8/10** — Jassy is the right leader for the AI transition. He built AWS, understands the technology deeply, and has demonstrated operational discipline (margins doubled under his watch). The risk is that retail innovation takes a back seat to cloud, but the market is pricing Amazon as an AI/cloud story anyway.
