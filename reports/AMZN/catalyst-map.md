# AMZN Catalyst Map 🎯

*Amazon.com, Inc. | Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## 1. AWS AI Revenue Acceleration (Q1-Q4 2026)

- **What:** AWS is the #1 cloud provider and primary AI workload channel. Bedrock (managed AI models), Trainium2/3 custom chips, and SageMaker drive AI-specific revenue. AWS likely exceeded $110B annualized by Q4 2025.
- **When:** Each quarterly earnings (late April, late July, late October 2026)
- **Impact:** AWS re-accelerating to 25%+ growth would add $100-150B market cap (+5-7%)
- **Probability:** Medium-High (capacity-constrained, not demand-constrained)
- **Market Expectation:** ~19% AWS growth, ~$130B+ FY2026 revenue
- **Our Edge:** Massive 2025 capex (~$75B+) unlocks capacity in 2026; Trainium chips become viable NVIDIA alternatives for inference; demand backlog could surprise

## 2. Anthropic Partnership Value (2026)

- **What:** Amazon invested $8B+ in Anthropic (Claude). As Anthropic scales commercially, investment appreciates and deepens AWS-Anthropic integration
- **When:** Potential Anthropic funding round or revenue milestones through 2026
- **Impact:** Mark-to-market gains $5-15B; strategic moat deepens
- **Probability:** High
- **Market Expectation:** Largely priced at investment level; revenue contribution underappreciated
- **Our Edge:** Anthropic emerging as enterprise AI model of choice — makes AWS default AI cloud for safety-focused enterprise

## 3. Advertising Revenue Acceleration (Q1-Q4 2026)

- **What:** Amazon ads ~$65B+ annual run-rate, 3rd largest digital ad platform. AI-powered targeting + Prime Video ads expanding TAM
- **When:** Each quarterly report
- **Impact:** 20%+ growth sustains high-margin revenue mix shift (+2-3% op margin lift)
- **Probability:** High
- **Market Expectation:** ~20% ad growth priced in
- **Our Edge:** Prime Video ad tier still ramping globally; gen-AI ad creative tools launching 2026

## 4. Margin Expansion (Full Year 2026)

- **What:** Op margin: 6.4% (2023) → 10.75% (2024) → 11.16% (2025). Consensus expects 12-13% in 2026
- **When:** Continuous, each quarter
- **Impact:** Each 1% margin on $821B revenue = $8.2B operating income = ~$0.60 EPS
- **Probability:** High
- **Our Edge:** Robotics/automation (750K+ robots), Trainium cost savings could push above consensus

## 5. Project Kuiper Satellite Internet (H2 2026)

- **What:** $10B+ satellite internet constellation. Commercial service expected late 2026
- **When:** H2 2026 commercial beta
- **Impact:** Narrative catalyst; $100B+ TAM long-term optionality
- **Probability:** Medium (execution risk)
- **Our Edge:** Optionality not priced; reframes Amazon as infrastructure titan

## 6. Post-Q4 Earnings Gap-Down Entry (Current)

- **What:** Q4 2025: $213.4B revenue (+13.6% YoY), FY2025 EPS $7.17. Stock dropped from ~$230 to ~$205 post-earnings
- **When:** Now — gap to fill
- **Impact:** Technical entry at $211 support
- **Our Edge:** Weakness is macro/tariff-driven, not fundamental deterioration

## 7. Trainium3 Chip Launch (H2 2026)

- **What:** Custom AI training chip competing with NVIDIA for internal AWS workloads
- **When:** H2 2026
- **Impact:** Saves billions in NVIDIA costs; improves AWS margins 1-2%
- **Probability:** Medium-High
- **Our Edge:** Trainium2 already in production; inference viability by late 2026

## 8. Capital Return / Buyback (2026)

- **What:** With $77.7B net income, formal buyback possible
- **When:** Any earnings call
- **Impact:** $20B program = +3-5% signal
- **Probability:** Medium (FCF compressed by capex)
