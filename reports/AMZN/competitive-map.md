# AMZN Competitive Map 🗺️

*Amazon.com, Inc. | Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## Who Benefits from the SAME Catalysts?

### AI Cloud Infrastructure Boom
- **MSFT** (Azure + OpenAI) — direct competitor, but AI rising tide lifts both
- **GOOGL** (GCP + Gemini) — same dynamic, smaller cloud share
- **NVDA** — sells GPUs to ALL cloud providers including AWS
- **AVGO** — custom ASICs for hyperscalers (including Amazon's competitors)

### E-commerce / Advertising
- **GOOGL** — search ads benefit from same digital shift
- **META** — social ads, also AI-powered targeting
- **SHOP** — Shopify merchants rely on Amazon marketplace AND compete with it

## Who Loses if Amazon Wins?

### Cloud
- **ORCL** — Oracle Cloud trying to compete; Amazon winning AI workloads hurts Oracle's cloud growth narrative
- **IBM** — legacy cloud/consulting losing to AWS automation
- **Traditional IT** (HPE, DELL servers) — enterprises shifting to cloud reduces on-prem spend

### E-commerce
- **WMT** — Walmart e-commerce growing but Amazon dominates Prime loyalty
- **TGT, COST** — traditional retail market share erosion
- **BABA (US)** — Amazon vs. Alibaba for global e-commerce; both compete for international markets

### Advertising
- **SNAP, PINS** — smaller ad platforms lose budget share to Amazon's high-intent shopping ads

## Pair Trade Opportunities

### Long AMZN / Short ORCL
- **Thesis:** AWS AI growth accelerates while Oracle Cloud struggles to compete at scale
- **Risk:** Oracle has strong database franchise that's defensible

### Long AMZN / Short WMT
- **Thesis:** Amazon's e-commerce + ads + cloud triple engine vs. Walmart's single-vector retail
- **Risk:** WMT has grocery moat and physical store network

### Long AMZN / Long NVDA
- **Thesis:** Not a pair trade but a basket — both win from AI infrastructure buildout
- **Risk:** Correlated downside in AI sentiment reversal

## Competitive Moats

| Moat | Strength | Threat |
|------|----------|--------|
| AWS cloud leadership | Very strong (33% market share) | Azure gaining; multi-cloud reduces lock-in |
| Prime ecosystem (200M+ members) | Very strong | Hard to replicate |
| Logistics network | Very strong | Cost to build is moat itself |
| Advertising data (shopping intent) | Strong | Google/Meta have different but strong data |
| Trainium/custom chips | Emerging | NVIDIA still dominant |
