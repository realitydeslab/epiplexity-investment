## Free Cash Flow Concern

- **FCF 2025:** $7.7B (down 77% from $32.9B in 2024)
- **Reason:** Massive capex for AI infrastructure (~$75B+ in 2025)
- **Interpretation:** This is INVESTMENT, not deterioration. Amazon is building AI capacity that will generate returns 2026+
- **Risk:** If AI capex doesn't generate proportional returns, FCF remains compressed

## AWS Growth (Key Segment)

AWS is the profit engine (~60%+ of operating income):
- FY2025 AWS revenue estimated ~$107-110B (19-22% growth)
- AI workloads growing fastest within AWS
- Capacity constraints being resolved through 2026
- Backlog of committed cloud spend likely $150B+
