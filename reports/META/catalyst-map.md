# META — Catalyst Map 🎯

*Research Date: March 6, 2026 | Current Price: $645.70*

## Catalyst 1: Q1 2026 Earnings (Late April 2026)
- **What:** First quarter results showing AI monetization traction. Consensus expects ~$36-38B revenue. Key metric: ad revenue per user uplift from AI-powered ad targeting.
- **When:** Late April 2026 (likely April 29)
- **Impact:** +/- 8-12%. Q4 2025 beat by 8.13% on EPS and 2.45% on sales.
- **Probability:** High (Meta has beaten EPS estimates consistently)
- **Market Expectation:** ~$30.55 full-year EPS consensus for 2026.
- **Our Edge:** Market underestimates the AI ad revenue flywheel. Llama integration into ad targeting drives superior ROAS — shows up as higher CPMs and ad load.

## Catalyst 2: Llama 4 / Next-Gen Model Release (Q2-Q3 2026)
- **What:** Next-generation open-source AI model. Meta created a new "applied AI engineering organization" (March 4, 2026) to accelerate model deployment — signals imminent major release.
- **When:** Q2-Q3 2026
- **Impact:** +5-10%. Establishes Meta as AI platform leader, drives developer ecosystem.
- **Probability:** High (org restructuring confirms priority)
- **Market Expectation:** Expected but timing uncertain. Not priced as a catalyst.
- **Our Edge:** New AI unit structure = operationalizing AI faster. Model releases paired with product launches (AI shopping, chatbot monetization).

## Catalyst 3: AI Shopping / Commerce Launch (Q2-Q3 2026)
- **What:** AI-powered shopping research tool in testing (Bloomberg, March 3, 2026). Rivals Google Shopping and Amazon product search. Deployed across Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp.
- **When:** Beta Q2 2026, broader rollout Q3 2026
- **Impact:** +8-15% if successful. Opens new TAM: product discovery → transaction → ad attribution loop.
- **Probability:** Medium-High (already in testing)
- **Market Expectation:** Barely priced in. Street focused on core ads, not commerce transformation.
- **Our Edge:** 3.3B+ DAP. Converting even 2-3% to commercial intent via AI = massive incremental revenue.

## Catalyst 4: WhatsApp Business AI Monetization (Throughout 2026)
- **What:** AI-powered business messaging at scale. Click-to-WhatsApp ads = fastest-growing ad format. AI chatbots for customer service/sales.
- **When:** Gradual, key metrics in Q2-Q3 earnings
- **Impact:** +5-8% as market realizes WhatsApp is a $10B+ revenue business
- **Probability:** High (already ramping)
- **Market Expectation:** Acknowledged but undermodeled (<5% of revenue in most models).
- **Our Edge:** In emerging markets, WhatsApp IS the internet. Zero competition in business messaging.

## Catalyst 5: Meta AI Assistant 1B+ Users (H2 2026)
- **What:** Meta AI chatbot across all apps reaching critical mass. Becomes platform for commerce, search, services — competing with Google Search.
- **When:** H2 2026 milestone
- **Impact:** +5-10% narrative shift — revalued as AI platform, not just social media
- **Probability:** Medium-High
- **Market Expectation:** Not modeled as standalone business
- **Our Edge:** Only AI assistant with native distribution across 3.3B DAP. Zero acquisition cost.

## Catalyst 6: Capital Return Acceleration
- **What:** $2.10/share TTM dividend (ex-date March 16, 2026), ~$60B+ annual buybacks. Shares declining ~1.5%/yr.
- **When:** Ongoing
- **Impact:** +2-3% floor support
- **Probability:** Very High
- **Our Edge:** At $46B FCF, buyback capacity may expand in H2 2026.

## Catalyst 7: Child Safety Legislation Risk ⚠️
- **What:** House panel advancing 12-bill online child safety package (March 6, 2026).
- **When:** H1-H2 2026
- **Impact:** -3-5% on headline risk
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Our Edge:** Meta proactively implementing teen safety features. Revenue impact likely minimal.
