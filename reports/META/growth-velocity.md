## Guidance vs Actual History
Meta has beaten EPS estimates in the last reported quarter by **8.13%** and revenue by **2.45%**. The company has a strong history of conservative guidance followed by upside surprises.

## User/Customer Growth
- Daily Active People (DAP) across family of apps: 3.35B+ (Q4 2025)
- Still growing in emerging markets (India, Southeast Asia, Africa)
- User engagement increasing with AI features (Reels, Meta AI assistant)

## Unit Economics
- Revenue per DAP: ~$60/year globally, $200+ in North America
- AI-driven ad targeting increasing ARPU without proportional cost increases
- Gross margins expanding as AI scales (82% in 2025 vs 78% in 2022)

## Path to Higher Margins
Already highly profitable. The question is whether AI capex ($60-65B in 2026) will translate to proportional revenue. Early signs say yes — revenue growth is accelerating even as capex grows.

## Growth Velocity Score: 9/10
Revenue accelerating at $200B+ scale. Margins stable. AI monetization inflecting. Rare combination.
