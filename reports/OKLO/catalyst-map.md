# OKLO — Catalyst Map 🎯

**Company:** Oklo Inc. (NYSE: OKLO)
**Date:** 2026-03-07
**Current Price:** ~$58.29

## Catalyst 1: Aurora-INL Criticality Achievement
- **What:** First criticality of the Aurora powerhouse at Idaho National Laboratory — the single most important technical milestone proving the reactor works
- **When:** Mid-2026 (DOE Reactor Pilot Program target)
- **Impact:** +40-60%. Would validate the entire technology stack and de-risk from "science project" to "proven reactor."
- **Probability:** Medium. Groundbreaking Sep 2025, borehole drilling complete, construction underway. But nuclear timelines historically slip.
- **Market expectation:** Skeptical — consensus sees delays into 2027+. Simply Wall St (Jan 2026) noted "prolonged NRC timeline reshaping investment story."
- **Our edge:** DOE Reactor Pilot Program provides a parallel, faster authorization pathway vs. traditional NRC licensing. Three Oklo projects selected. If DOE pathway delivers on time, market is underpricing this.

## Catalyst 2: NRC COLA Submission
- **What:** Formal Combined Operating License Application submission to NRC
- **When:** 2025-2026 (pre-application readiness engagement ongoing)
- **Impact:** +15-25%. Submission is a credibility milestone, signals regulatory maturity
- **Probability:** High. PDC topical report accepted for accelerated NRC review (Sep 2025).
- **Market expectation:** Partially priced in — market knows COLA coming but uncertain on timing
- **Our edge:** NRC acceptance of PDC under "accelerated timeline" suggests regulator more cooperative than market assumes

## Catalyst 3: Additional Customer Power Agreements
- **What:** New data center / industrial PPAs beyond existing ~14 GW pipeline (Switch 12 GW, Equinix 500 MW, RPower partnership)
- **When:** Rolling through 2026
- **Impact:** +10-20% per major deal
- **Probability:** High. AI data center power demand acute and growing.
- **Market expectation:** Discounts as "non-binding" / long-dated
- **Our edge:** Equinix 500 MW (Aug 2025) and Switch 12 GW show Tier 1 customers are serious

## Catalyst 4: HALEU Fuel Supply Resolution
- **What:** Securing commercial HALEU fuel — currently the biggest bottleneck
- **When:** 2026-2027
- **Impact:** +20-30% if resolved. Removes single largest execution risk.
- **Probability:** Medium-Low. Management concerned about commercial HALEU before 2030s. Fuel recycling facility got NRC approval Jan 2026.
- **Market expectation:** Major bear case — no fuel, no reactors
- **Our edge:** Vertically integrated fuel strategy (recycling + fresh HALEU + plutonium bridge fuel) provides multiple paths. Jan 2026 NRC fuel recycling approval underappreciated.

## Catalyst 5: DOE Reactor Pilot Program Milestones
- **What:** Continued DOE backing for Aurora-INL, Atomic Alchemy, Pluto test reactor
- **When:** Ongoing 2026
- **Impact:** +10-15%. Government backing de-risks and accelerates
- **Probability:** High. Three projects selected. Trump admin explicitly pro-nuclear. DOE Secretary Burgum attended groundbreaking.
- **Market expectation:** Partially priced in
- **Our edge:** Political tailwind underestimated
