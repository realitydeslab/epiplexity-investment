# OKLO — Leader Profile 👤

## Jacob DeWitte — Assessment

**Background:** Nuclear engineer, MIT-educated, co-founded Oklo in 2013. Has been the face of the company through its SPAC merger (2024) and public market journey.

**Strengths:**
- Deep technical expertise in fast reactor design
- Successfully navigated DOE partnerships and secured three Reactor Pilot Program selections
- Built relationships with Tier 1 customers (Switch, Equinix)
- Scaled Aurora design from 1.5 MWe → 15 MWe → 50-75 MWe, showing market responsiveness

**Concerns:**
- First NRC application was rejected in 2022 — had to restart the process
- Company remains pre-revenue after 13 years
- Aggressive public timelines that frequently slip

**Verdict:** Technically capable visionary, but execution risk is real. The DOE pathway pivot shows strategic adaptability.
