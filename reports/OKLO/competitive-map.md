# OKLO — Competitive Map 🗺️

**Date:** 2026-03-07

---

## Direct Competitors (Advanced Nuclear / SMR)

### NuScale Power (NYSE: SMR)
- **Only** SMR developer with NRC design certification (approved 2023)
- Light water reactor, 77 MWe per module
- **Advantage over Oklo:** Regulatory approval already in hand
- **Disadvantage:** UAMPS project cancelled (2023), struggling to find first customer
- **Stock:** ~$25-30 range
- **Verdict:** Further along regulatory but behind on customer pipeline

### Kairos Power (Private)
- Fluoride salt-cooled reactor, backed by Google
- DOE demonstration reactor at ORNL
- Private — not investable directly
- **Key competitor** for data center PPAs

### TerraPower (Private, Bill Gates)
- Natrium sodium-cooled reactor (similar tech to Oklo's Aurora)
- Wyoming demonstration plant under construction
- Backed by Bill Gates personally
- Private — not directly investable
- **Most direct technology competitor** to Oklo

### X-energy (Private)
- High-temperature gas reactor (Xe-100)
- DOE Advanced Reactor Demonstration Program funding
- Dow Chemical partnership
- SPAC merger attempted, cancelled

## Pair Trade Opportunities

### Long OKLO / Short SMR (NuScale)
- **Thesis:** If advanced nuclear succeeds, Oklo has better customer pipeline (14 GW vs. NuScale's ~0) and a more innovative reactor design. NuScale has regulatory approval but no customers.
- **Risk:** NuScale's NRC approval is real; Oklo's is years away

### Long LEU + OKLO (Basket)
- **Thesis:** Win regardless of which reactor wins — LEU supplies the fuel everyone needs
- **Better risk-adjusted play** than OKLO alone

### Long CEG / Short OKLO
- **Thesis:** Constellation has existing, operating nuclear plants generating revenue NOW. Oklo is speculative. If nuclear power demand rises, CEG captures it immediately.
- **Conservative way** to play nuclear renaissance

| Factor | Oklo | NuScale | TerraPower |

| Customer Pipeline | ✅ 14+ GW | ❌ Weak | ⚠️ Moderate |
