# OKLO — Growth Velocity 📈

**Status:** Pre-revenue company
**Date:** 2026-03-07

---

## Revenue Growth: N/A — Pre-Revenue

Oklo has generated **$0 revenue** since inception. This is a pre-revenue, pre-commercial nuclear developer. Revenue is not expected until first reactor achieves commercial operation (late 2027-2028 at earliest).

**This is not a growth stock — it's a binary bet on technology commercialization.**

---

## Customer Pipeline Growth (Proxy for Revenue Trajectory)

| Period | Cumulative Customer Commitments |
|--------|-------------------------------|
| Q4 2024 | ~2 GW (early LOIs) |
| Q1 2025 | ~14 GW (Switch 12 GW master agreement) |
| Q3 2025 | ~14.5 GW (Equinix 500 MW added) |

**Pipeline is growing** — 14+ GW of customer interest is massive for a company with zero operational reactors. However:
- These are non-binding agreements / master frameworks
- Actual deployment depends on reactors being built, licensed, and fueled
- Conversion rate from pipeline to real PPAs is uncertain

## Balance Sheet Strength

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Cash + Marketable Securities (Q3 2025) | ~$1.2 billion |
| FY2025 Cash Burn Guidance | $65-80M |
| Runway at Current Burn Rate | ~15-18 years |
| Capital Raised (ATM, 2025) | $540M |

**The balance sheet is a fortress.** $1.2B in cash against $65-80M annual burn gives ~15+ years of runway. This eliminates near-term dilution/bankruptcy risk.

However: the $540M ATM raise in 2025 was dilutive and occurred at higher prices. Management may do another capital raise if conditions favor it (bear concern from analysts).

## Guidance vs. Actual History

- FY2024 operating loss guidance: ~$40M → Actual: $52.8M (missed, partly due to SPAC accounting)
- FY2025 cash burn guidance: $65-80M → On track through Q3
- First commercial power timeline: Originally 2026 → Now late 2027 to early 2028 (slipped)
- **Pattern: timelines slip, costs rise.** Typical for nuclear but erodes credibility.

## Path to Profitability

**Estimated timeline:** 2029-2031 at earliest
1. First reactor criticality: Mid-2026 (target)
2. First commercial power: Late 2027-2028
3. Revenue ramp from multiple deployed reactors: 2028-2030
4. Profitability: 2030+ (requires multiple reactors + fuel recycling revenue)

**Key risk:** If Aurora-INL criticality slips to 2027-2028, profitability pushes to 2032+.

---

## Verdict

Growth velocity is **not applicable in traditional terms**. This is a capital-intensive, pre-revenue infrastructure play. The "growth" metric that matters is:
1. ✅ Customer pipeline expanding (14+ GW)
2. ✅ Balance sheet strong ($1.2B cash)
3. ⚠️ Losses accelerating (expected but watch the rate)
4. ❌ Timelines slipping (first power moved from 2026 → 2027-2028)
5. ❌ Zero revenue, zero proof of commercial viability
