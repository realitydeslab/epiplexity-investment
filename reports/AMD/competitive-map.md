## The Real Competitive Dynamic

AMD occupies a unique position: **the value/efficiency challenger**

- **NVIDIA** owns training and the CUDA ecosystem (86% share)
- **AMD** is winning inference (30% better tokens-per-dollar) and offers an open alternative (ROCm)
- **Custom ASICs** (Google TPU, Amazon Trainium, Meta MTIA) threaten BOTH AMD and NVIDIA
- **The risk:** Hyperscalers build their own chips and neither AMD nor NVIDIA wins

## Market Share Trend

- AMD DC GPU share: ~10% in 2024 → ~14% in 2025 → targeting 20%+ in 2026
- AMD server CPU share: ~25% in 2024 → ~35% in 2025 → targeting 40%+ in 2026
- NVIDIA DC GPU share: 90% (2024) → 86% (2026) — declining but still dominant
