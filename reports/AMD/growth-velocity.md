## Growth Targets vs Reality

- **Management target:** >35% revenue CAGR through 2028
- **FY2025 actual:** ~32% YoY — slightly below the 35% compound target but Q3/Q4 acceleration suggests 2026 will exceed it
- **Data center target:** >60% CAGR — Q4 DC was +39% YoY, needs MI450 ramp to hit 60%
- **Beat/miss history:** AMD has beaten consensus in 8 of last 10 quarters. Management guides conservatively.

## Profitability

- **Non-GAAP gross margin:** 54% (Q3 2025) — stable, room for expansion as MI450 (higher ASP) ramps
- **Non-GAAP operating margin:** 24% (Q3 2025) — management targeting 35%+ by 2028
- **Non-GAAP EPS:** $1.20/Q3 (+30% YoY). FY2025 ~$4.50-5.00. Target: $20+ by 2028.
- **Path to profitability inflection:** Already profitable. The story is margin EXPANSION — from 24% to 35%+ operating margin as software/AI mix increases.

## Unit Economics

- **AI GPU ASPs rising:** MI355X and MI450 carry higher margins than prior-gen MI300X
- **Inference advantage:** MI355X delivers 30% faster inference than B200 at ~40% better tokens-per-dollar — drives competitive win rate
- **EPYC Turin:** Higher core counts = higher ASPs, displacing cheaper Intel Xeons

## Order Backlog

- **$50B+ design win backlog** — largest in AMD history
- **6GW OpenAI/Meta capacity commitment** — multi-year, potentially tens of billions
- **Backlog conversion risk:** CoWoS advanced packaging at TSMC is the bottleneck

## Forward Estimates

- **2026E revenue:** ~$44-48B (consensus ~$45B, representing ~30-35% growth)
- **2026E EPS:** ~$6.50-7.50 non-GAAP
- **2027E EPS:** ~$11 non-GAAP (Seeking Alpha analyst estimate)
- **Forward P/E:** ~29-33x on 2027E EPS — reasonable for a 35% grower
