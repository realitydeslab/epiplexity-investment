# AMD — Leader Profile 👤

*Research date: March 6, 2026*

---

## CEO: Dr. Lisa Su

- **Role:** Chair, President & CEO since 2014
- **Track record:** Transformed AMD from near-bankruptcy to $216B+ market cap. Revenue grew from ~$5B (2014) to $34.6B (2025). One of the most respected tech CEOs globally.
- **Execution credibility:** HIGH. Delivered on Zen architecture roadmap, EPYC market share gains, and now AI GPU pivot — all on time. Her Analyst Day targets have historically been met or exceeded.
- **Strategic vision:** "AI Everywhere" — positioning AMD across training (MI450), inference (MI355X), edge (Ryzen AI), and server CPU (EPYC Turin). Not trying to out-NVIDIA NVIDIA; carving the value/efficiency niche.
- **Compensation aligned:** Significant equity stake (4.1M+ shares, ~$885M at current price).

## Insider Activity

- **Lisa Su:** Sold 125,000 shares on Feb 11, 2026 at ~$210-219/share under pre-arranged 10b5-1 plan (adopted Sep 9, 2025). This is ROUTINE — she sells on a regular schedule for diversification. Pattern consistent with prior years ($142-165 range sales in 2024).
- **Signal:** NEUTRAL. Planned sales, not discretionary. She still holds 4.1M+ shares worth ~$885M — skin in the game is massive.
- **No unusual insider buying detected** — but given the stock's run, this isn't unusual for a company this size.

## Key Hires/Departures (Last 6 Months)

- No major C-suite departures — leadership team stable.
- Continued aggressive hiring in ROCm/software engineering to close CUDA gap.
- AI data center sales team expanded for hyperscaler relationships.

## Public Statements (Near-Term Outlook)

- Q4 2025 call (Feb 2, 2026): "2025 was a defining year... AI products for data centers seeing rapid adoption."
- Guided to >35% revenue CAGR through 2028, with data center at >60% CAGR.
- MI450 described as "on track" for H2 2026 — no delays signaled.
- Called $50B+ design win backlog "the largest in AMD history."

## Assessment

Lisa Su is arguably the best semiconductor CEO operating today. She's navigated AMD through multiple cycles and consistently under-promises/over-delivers. The risk isn't leadership — it's whether MI450 execution and CoWoS packaging supply can match the ambition.

**Leadership grade: A+**
