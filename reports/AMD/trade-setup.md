- MI450 launches on time, early benchmarks show parity/superiority vs Vera Rubin in inference
- Q1-Q3 2026 all beat-and-raise
- OpenAI/Meta revenue starts flowing at scale
- Forward P/E of 30x on $11 2027E EPS = $330

### Base Case ($270)
- MI450 on track but limited H2 revenue contribution
- DC revenue grows ~40% YoY (below 60% target but still strong)

### Bear Case ($165)
- MI450 delayed to 2027 due to CoWoS constraints
- NVIDIA Vera Rubin launch steals thunder
- Custom ASICs from Google/Amazon take share
- 22x P/E on $7.50 2026E EPS = $165

## Risk Management

- **Stop-loss:** $180 (-17%) — below 200 DMA and major support
- **Position sizing:** 5-8% of portfolio — high conviction but semiconductor volatility is real
- **What kills the trade:**
  1. MI450 delay beyond Q4 2026
  2. NVIDIA announces aggressive pricing that erodes AMD's value proposition
  3. Custom ASIC adoption accelerates faster than expected (Google TPU, Amazon Trainium)
  4. CoWoS packaging bottleneck limits all GPU shipments
- **Correlation risk:** Highly correlated with NVDA, SOX index, and AI spending narrative. A broad AI spending pullback would hit AMD regardless of execution.
