# AMD (Advanced Micro Devices) — Catalyst Map 🎯

*Research date: March 6, 2026 | Current price: ~$216*

---

## Catalyst 1: MI450 GPU Launch & Ramp (H2 2026)

- **What:** AMD's next-gen Instinct MI450 AI accelerator — 2nm TSMC node, 40 PFLOPS FP4, 432GB HBM4 at 19.6 TB/s. Direct competitor to NVIDIA's Vera Rubin.
- **When:** H2 2026 (likely Q3/Q4 sampling, volume ramp Q4 2026 into Q1 2027)
- **Impact:** +30-50% stock upside if execution is clean. MI450 is AMD's chance to close the training gap with NVIDIA.
- **Probability:** HIGH — already on TSMC's 2nm allocation; AMD confirmed "on track" in Feb 2026 earnings.
- **Market expectation:** Consensus expects solid launch but limited share gain vs NVIDIA's entrenched CUDA ecosystem.
- **Our edge:** The 6GW OpenAI/Meta capacity commitment validates MI450 before it ships. ROCm maturity (native PyTorch/JAX) weakens the CUDA moat argument.

## Catalyst 2: Q1 2026 Earnings (Late April/Early May 2026)

- **What:** First full quarter post-MI355X ramp. Data center revenue expected ~$6B+ (from $5.4B in Q4 2025).
- **When:** Late April 2026
- **Impact:** +10-15% on beat-and-raise; -10-15% on miss.
- **Probability:** HIGH — AMD has beaten in 8 of last 10 quarters.
- **Market expectation:** Street at ~$10.5B total revenue. DC segment $5.8-6.2B.
- **Our edge:** MI355X inference advantage (30% better tokens-per-dollar vs B200) likely underestimated.

## Catalyst 3: OpenAI/Meta 6GW GPU Capacity Deal Revenue Recognition

- **What:** Landmark agreement to supply 6GW of GPU capacity — potentially tens of billions in multi-year revenue.
- **When:** Revenue ramp throughout 2026, accelerating H2.
- **Impact:** +20-30% if backlog quantified in guidance.
- **Probability:** HIGH — deal announced; execution is the variable.
- **Our edge:** $50B+ design win backlog not fully modeled into consensus.

## Catalyst 4: EPYC Turin Server CPU Share Gains

- **What:** EPYC Turin (Zen 5) taking share from Intel. Server CPU share ~35% and climbing.
- **When:** Ongoing throughout 2026.
- **Impact:** +5-10% incremental; steady earnings builder.
- **Probability:** HIGH — Intel recovery slow, Turin reviews excellent.

## Catalyst 5: ROCm Software Ecosystem Maturation

- **What:** ROCm has native PyTorch/JAX support. ZLUDA project enables drop-in CUDA compatibility.
- **When:** Continuous through 2026.
- **Impact:** Qualitative narrative shift from "hardware good, software lags" to "full stack competitor."
- **Probability:** MEDIUM-HIGH.

## Catalyst 6: Gaming Segment Recovery

- **What:** Gaming hit $843M in Q4 2025 (+50% YoY). Console refresh + RDNA 4.
- **When:** Through 2026.
- **Impact:** +3-5%; earnings diversification.
- **Probability:** MEDIUM.
