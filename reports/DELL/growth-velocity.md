## Unit Economics
- **Gross margin:** 22.2% in FY2025, likely compressed in FY2026 due to AI server mix (lower margin than PCs/storage). Key risk.
- **Operating income:** $6.2B FY2025, growing to ~$8B+ in FY2026 est.
- **Operating cash flow:** $4.5B FY2025 (down 48% due to working capital for AI servers). Recovered to $11B+ in FY2026.
- **AI server margins:** Lower than traditional servers (~15-18% gross vs 25-30%), but IMPROVING as Dell adds storage, networking, services.

## Path to Profitability Assessment
Dell is already very profitable. The question is margin trajectory:
- **Bull case:** Enterprise AI mix increases, services attach rate rises, gross margin stabilizes at 22-23%
- **Base case:** AI server volume growth offsets margin pressure, gross margin ~21-22%
- **Bear case:** Hyperscaler price wars compress margins to 19-20%, revenue grows but profits flat

## Verdict: ACCELERATING ✅
Revenue growth is clearly accelerating (6% → 39% sequential quarters). AI orders are parabolic ($64.1B FY2026 total). The only concern is margin mix — but $11B+ cash flow generation provides buffer.
