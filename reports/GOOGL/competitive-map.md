# GOOGL Competitive Map — Alphabet/Google

*Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## Who Benefits from the SAME Catalysts?

### AI Cloud Expansion
- **MSFT** — Azure AI, OpenAI partnership. Direct cloud competitor.
- **AMZN** — AWS Bedrock, Trainium chips. Cloud #1 by market share.
- **NVDA** — Google's $75B+ GPU spend benefits NVIDIA directly.
- **AVGO** — Google's custom TPU partner for silicon design.

### AI Model Arms Race
- **META** — Llama open-source models compete with Gemini
- **MSFT/OpenAI** — GPT models vs Gemini
- **Anthropic (private, AMZN-backed)** — Claude vs Gemini

### Robotaxi/Autonomous
- **TSLA** — FSD robotaxi competing with Waymo
- **GM/Cruise** — Re-entering autonomous after setbacks
- **Uber/Lyft** — Waymo partnerships AND competition

## Who Loses if Google Wins?

### Search AI Dominance
- **Perplexity (private)** — AI search startup; Google AI Overviews undercuts their raison d'être
- **MSFT/Bing** — If Gemini-powered Search maintains dominance, Bing stays irrelevant
- **Traditional SEO companies** — AI Overviews reduce clickthrough to organic results

### Cloud Market Share Gains
- **ORCL** — Oracle Cloud trying to win AI workloads
- **IBM** — Watson AI increasingly irrelevant vs Gemini/GPT/Claude

### Waymo Wins = Others Lose
- **Uber/Lyft** — Long-term robotaxi disruption
- **Traditional auto** — Reduced car ownership

## Pair Trade Opportunities

### 1. Long GOOGL / Short Bing-dependent ad plays
- Google's AI search moat widens. Companies dependent on Bing search traffic lose.

### 2. Long GOOGL + AVGO (Google's custom chip ecosystem)
- AVGO designs Google's TPU chips. Both benefit from Google's $175-185B capex.
- Source: Schwab Network notes AVGO-GOOGL partnership strength

### 3. Long GOOGL / Short high-P/E AI pure-plays
- GOOGL at 26x forward P/E is cheaper than many AI plays with less revenue/profit.

## Key Competitive Dynamics

1. **Cloud War:** Google Cloud growing 48% vs Azure ~30% and AWS ~20%. Google is gaining share fastest.
2. **AI Model Race:** Gemini competitive but not clearly ahead of GPT-5 or Claude. Distribution advantage (750M MAUs, Android, Chrome) is the moat.
3. **Advertising:** Google still captures ~28% of global digital ad spend. META is main competitor. AI making ads more efficient = tailwind for both.
4. **Autonomous:** Waymo has clear lead in deployed robotaxis over Tesla FSD (which still requires driver attention in most markets).
