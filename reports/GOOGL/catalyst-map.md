# GOOGL Catalyst Map — Alphabet/Google

*Research Date: March 6, 2026*

## 1. Google Cloud Hyper-Growth Continuation (Q1-Q4 2026)

- **What:** Google Cloud revenue grew 48% YoY in Q4 2025 to $17.66B, with backlog doubling YoY to $240B. Cloud is now the fastest-growing major segment.
- **When:** Each quarterly earnings (Q1: ~Apr 29, Q2: ~Jul 28, Q3: ~Oct 28)
- **Impact:** +5-10% per beat. If Cloud crosses $100B annual run rate in 2026, stock re-rates.
- **Probability:** High — backlog is locked in, enterprise AI migration is structural
- **Market expectation:** Consensus expects ~20% total revenue growth for FY2026 ($485.6B). Cloud expected to maintain 35-45% growth.
- **Our edge:** Market still prices GOOGL primarily as an ad company (P/E 27.7 vs MSFT ~35). Cloud margin expansion + backlog suggest growth re-rating is coming.

## 2. Gemini AI Platform Scale (Ongoing 2026)

- **What:** Gemini app at 750M+ MAUs (up from 650M in Q3). Integration across Search, YouTube, Workspace, Cloud. Serving costs fell 78% in 2025.
- **When:** Google I/O (May 2026) will showcase next Gemini releases
- **Impact:** +3-5% on major launches. AI Overviews monetizing at par with traditional search = hugely bullish.
- **Probability:** High — already at scale
- **Market expectation:** Priced in as competitive; not priced in as potentially dominant
- **Our edge:** 78% cost reduction in serving = massive margin expansion as AI scales. Underappreciated.

## 3. $175-185B Capex Program (FY 2026)

- **What:** Guided 2026 capex at $175-185B, >2x 2025 spend. Massive AI compute, data centers, TPU investment.
- **When:** Throughout 2026; ROI visibility by H2
- **Impact:** Double-edged: short-term margin pressure, long-term moat. Stock dropped 3% on announcement.
- **Probability:** High — committed
- **Market expectation:** Nervous about spending
- **Our edge:** Cloud backlog ($240B) validates demand. This is the hyperscaler capex cycle — demand is real.

## 4. DOJ Antitrust Remedy Decision (Mid-2026)

- **What:** DOJ antitrust case remedy phase. Outcomes range from behavioral remedies (manageable) to forced Chrome/Android divestiture (nuclear).
- **When:** Remedy decision expected Q2-Q3 2026
- **Impact:** -10-15% worst case (divestiture); +5-8% if behavioral only
- **Probability:** Severe remedies: Low-Medium. Trump DOJ friendlier to big tech.
- **Market expectation:** Largely discounted worst case
- **Our edge:** Even Chrome divestiture isn't existential — Google Search dominance comes from quality, not bundling.

## 5. Waymo Scaling & Potential Spin-off (2026)

- **What:** 15M trips in 2025, 5+ US markets, Miami launch Jan 2026, $16B valuation round.
- **When:** New city launches throughout 2026; IPO discussions possible
- **Impact:** +3-5% on expansion news. IPO/spin-off could unlock $30-50B+ value.
- **Probability:** New cities: High. IPO: Medium
- **Market expectation:** Waymo value not reflected in GOOGL — buried in "Other Bets" losses ($3.6B Q4 loss)
- **Our edge:** At 15M trips, Waymo is approaching commercial viability. Clear US robotaxi leader.

## 6. Dividend Growth & Buybacks (2026)

- **What:** Dividend increased 38% to $0.83/share in 2025. Shares declining 1.7%/yr. $126.8B cash, $73.3B FCF.
- **When:** Dividend increase at Q1 earnings (April)
- **Impact:** +1-2% on increase. Signals capital discipline.
- **Probability:** High

## 7. Google I/O 2026 (May 2026)

- **What:** Annual developer conference — major Gemini updates, Android AI, new products
- **When:** May 2026
- **Impact:** +3-8% if announcements exceed expectations
- **Probability:** High for significant announcements
