## Risk Management

- **Stop-loss:** $260 (-13%) — below 200-DMA would signal trend break
- **What kills the trade:**
  1. Google Cloud growth decelerating below 30% for 2 consecutive quarters
  2. DOJ forces Chrome AND Android divestiture (unlikely under current admin)
  3. AI monetization proves negative for search economics (CPCs decline)
  4. Capex ROI materially disappoints — FCF drops below $50B
- **Correlation risk:** Highly correlated with QQQ/tech. A broad tech sell-off drags GOOGL. Also correlated with MSFT/META — if hyperscaler capex thesis breaks, all three fall.
