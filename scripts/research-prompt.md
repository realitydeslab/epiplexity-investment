# Epiplexity Investment Research Prompt

You are researching {TICKER} ({NAME}) for the Epiplexity Investment project.

## Your Task

Produce a comprehensive Future Report with three sections:

### 1. Vision Report
- What future is this company building?
- Read their latest earnings call transcript, annual report, investor presentations
- What is the CEO's stated 5-10 year vision?
- What strategic bets are they making (R&D, acquisitions, new markets)?
- What does the world look like if they succeed?

### 2. Leader Profile
- Who is the CEO/founder?
- Track record: previous companies, exits, achievements
- Insider buying/selling in last 12 months
- Public statements about company direction
- Leadership team — key lieutenants and their backgrounds
- Red flags: lawsuits, controversies, turnover

### 3. Feasibility Assessment
- TAM (Total Addressable Market) — how big is the opportunity?
- Moat: what stops competitors? (tech, network effects, brand, patents, regulatory)
- Financial health: revenue growth, margins, cash runway, debt
- Competitive landscape: who else is trying? how do they compare?
- Key risks ranked by severity
- Scenario modeling:
  - 🐂 Bull case (what goes right)
  - 📊 Base case (most likely)
  - 🐻 Bear case (what goes wrong)
- If I invest $10,000 today, what could it look like in 5 years under each scenario?

### Output Format
Write each section as a separate markdown file:
- vision-report.md
- leader-profile.md
- feasibility.md
- investment-thesis.md (synthesized 1-page thesis)

Use real data. Cite sources. Include dates. No speculation without labeling it as such.
